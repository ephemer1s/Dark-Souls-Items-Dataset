            Black Knight Greatsword is a weapon in Dark Souls 2.

             

            Greatsword wielded by knights who served a lord of light in a long-forgotten age.

            Even after their flesh was charred by flame, they remained as strong as ever, and stood watch, challenging visitors to their land. 

            Acquired From

            • Located behind illusory wall after Belfry Sol.
            • Rare drop from Kobold enemies at the entrance of Aldia's Keep.

             

            Hints and Tips:

            • Despite the clear reference to the Black Knight sword of the first Dark Souls, this weapon bears little resemblance to that weapon.
            • When you reach the ledge at the end of Belfry Sol where you can drop back down to the Belfry Sol bonfire there is a hidden wall to the right. There are 2 chests in the room. The sword is in the left chest.
            • As of Patch 1.03 this weapon can be buffed with spells.
            • One handed moveset is similiar to Claymore, While two handed moveset is same as Thorned Greatsword's two handed light attack, swinging speed is varied by light or strong attack.
            • Cannot use resins to buff weapon, however it is buffable with magic.
            • Considering the length and damage of this greatsword it's a viable option for STR/DEX fire caster builds, however it uses a sizable chunk of stamina for strong attacks. It can also knock down enemies with lunge attacks.
            • Two-handing this weapon alters your stance, allowing the wielder to leverage the weight of their body while striking; however, this greatly reduces the range of this weapon, which may cause inexperienced users to miss swings they expect to hit and leave themselves open to retaliation.
            • At Fire +5 with 40 STR/DEX, and 30 INT/FTH the Black Knight Greatsword does 268 + 81 Physical and 115 + 93 Fire Damage (AR 557).
            • The stats requirements in Scholar of the First Sin are different; you need 28 STR, 20 DEX, 8 INT (12 INT pre-SOTFS) and 12 FTH.
            • Used by Syan Soldiers wielding swords.

            Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity,Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.
            The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Light] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].
            This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".
            Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!" at this point the weapon does not perform at it's best.
            Weight: How much the item weights when equipped.
            Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit
            Attack Type: Defines what kind of swing set the weapon has.
