            Demon's Greataxe is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

             

            Carved from the bones of fellow demons. Wielded by the lesser Taurus demons."
            "This axe is imbued with no special power, but can merrily beat foes to a pulp, providing you have the strength to wield it."

            How to Get / Where to Find the Demon's Greataxe

            • Dropped by Taurus Demons
            • Sold by Shiva of the East for 10,000 Souls
            • Drops from Minor Taurus Demon (5% chance - Demon Ruins)

             

            Notes:

            • Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
            • At +15 with 35 Strength, this weapon has an AR of 661.
            • At Crystal +5 with 66 Strength, this weapon has AR of 728 when one-handed and 767 when two-handed.
            • A very solid pure Strength weapon, with S scaling for Strength.
