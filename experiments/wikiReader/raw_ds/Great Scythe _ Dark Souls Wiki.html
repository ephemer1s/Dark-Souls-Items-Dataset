            Great Scythe is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

            Weapon with a long curved blade. Converted from a wheat-harvesting tool.
            The magnificent sharp curved blade instills fear in opponents. Perhaps it is their survival instinct at work."

            How to Get / Where to Find the Great Scythe

            • Found in The Catacombs, above lever to the second closed door. The entrance is accessed by jumping off the second spiked rotating bridge.
            • Video Location Timestamp 19:05

             

             

            Notes

            • Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
            • Bleed build up is 30 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.
            • This weapon can be acquired very early in game, and there's a green titanite shard in the catacombs as well, giving you the option to go to blacksmith Vamos and make a fire scythe (the scythe must be upgraded to +5 first). Blacksmith Vamos sells titanite shards.
            • One-handed moveset is both very long ranged and versatile, and consumes relatively little stamina - thus it is very good for lightweight characters.
