            Tin Darkmoon Catalyst is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

            Catalyst born from the soul of the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, Darkmoon deity who watches over the abandoned city of the Gods, Anor Londo. Gwyndolin is Gwyn's lastborn and a legitimate god, but he is also a Moon sorcerer, and this wand is boosted by faith, not intelligence."

            How to Get / Where to Find the Tin Darkmoon Catalyst

            • Ascend: From any catalyst, along with Dark Sun Gwyndolin's soul, at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.

            Notes

            • Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
            • This catalyst scales Mag Adj according to your Faith stat.
            • To cast spells with it, you will still need the minimum Int requirement.

                                Unlike Velka's Talisman this one is actually reasonably usable, at 25 Intelligence and 50 Faith you get access to all of the Sorceries outside of Pursuers, both Soul Spear variants and White Dragon Breath, and they complement the Miracles as well. While they're generally weaker than the stronger Miracles you get more uses and faster casting speeds, plus you can get multiple copies of most of them in a single NG cycle.

                                Also if you're willing to go for Pursuers (32 int) or Soul Spear (36 int), it's only surpassed by the Tin Crystalization Catalyst and Manus Catalyst which halve your casts so those are only good if you're looking for nuking stuff and not general use.

                                Though I'd argue that 25 Intelligence is the ideal amount as you get four soulmasses with Homing Soulmass and Homing Crystal Soulmass and Crystal Magic Weapon which is a more versatile weapon buff than Darkmoon Blade despite being weaker (only applies in NG+) with more uses, it's duration being extended with the Lingering Dragoncrest Ring and the fact that it's not tied to any Covenants either, so you can use it and Sunlight Spear at the same time if you want to.
