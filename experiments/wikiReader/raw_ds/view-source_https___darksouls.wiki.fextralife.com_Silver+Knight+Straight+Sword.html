            Silver Knight Straight Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

            The silver knights of Anor Londo guard the city using this beautifully slender weapon.
            "Its chain attacks, in which the wielder takes great advancing steps and makes use of his bodyweight, are deadly even in single hits."

             

            How to Get / Where to Find the Silver Knight Straight Sword

            • Drops from Silver Knights (Sword and Shield) (2% Chance - Anor Londo).

             

            Hints and Tips:

            • Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 100 Souls.
            • Silver Knight Straight Sword can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
            • With 99 Strength and Dexterity and at +5, the attack rating of this sword will be 419.
            • This sword originally had a Divine modifier, but later game patches removed it and made the weapon enchantable instead.
            • Higher base damage compared to other straight swords. Low scaling and weight of 6.0 however make it less optimal choice for PvP than Balder Side Sword or Longsword.
            • You can true combo light attacks after a heavy R2 attack with this weapon, but not vice versa.

            Silver Knight Straight Sword Upgrade Table


            Unique Upgrades are performed by any Blacksmith or by using the Weapon Smithbox.
            Regular Reinforcement Scales 8% for Strength and 52% for Dexterity to increase your AR.
            To reach max, you need: 10,000 souls and 10 Twinkling Titanite.
