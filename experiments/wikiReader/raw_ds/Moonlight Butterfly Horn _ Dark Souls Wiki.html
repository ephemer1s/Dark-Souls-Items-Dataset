            Moonlight Butterfly Horn is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

            Weapon born from the mystical creature of the Darkroot Garden, the Moonlight Butterfly. The horns of the butterfly, a being created by Seath, are imbued with a pure magic power."

            How to Get / Where to Find the Moonlight Butterfly Horn

            • Ascension: Ascend a +10 Spear or Rapier class weapon with the Soul of the Moonlight Butterfly, at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.

            Notes:

            • Give to Frampt to receive 1000 souls.

            Lore

            • The Moonlight Butterfly Horn is part of a long tradition of using caduceus patterns in Souls games.

            Upgrade cost:

            • +1: 5,000 souls + 1 Demon Titanite
            • +2: 5,000 souls + 1 Demon Titanite
            • +3: 5,000 souls + 2 Demon Titanite
            • +4: 5,000 souls + 2 Demon Titanite
            • +5: 5,000 souls + 4 Demon Titanite

             

            Moonlight Butterfly Horn Upgrade Table

             Demon Titanite weapon upgrades are usually for Boss Soul Weapons, created at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.
            Regular Reinforcement Scales 100% for  Intelligence to increase your AR. Also, gives 100 critical bonus.
            To reach max, you need: 25,000 souls and 10    Demon Titanite.

                                                      If you're doing an INT-Build, this spear is a good (poor man's?) substitute until you get the Moonlight Great Sword. Or if you failed to cut off Seath's tail on your first playthrough. For one thing, you can get the Soul of the Butterfly much sooner and spears are purchaseable from the Undead Merchant (male). You still have to make it to Anor Londo, though and the Sword is available shortly after defeating Smough and Ornstein. The Horn being a spear has the advantage of being safer to use, as you can raise your shield and attack at the same time. This can be useful if your character is a bit more squishy. The Horn also has lower requirements with 12 STR and 14 INT. The Sword requires 16 STR, 10 DEX and 28 INT. Obviously, if you're not doing an INT-build, go for the shield.
