            Dragonslayer Spear is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

             

            Cross spear born from the soul of Ornstein, a Dragonslayer guarding Anor Londo cathedral.
            Inflicts lightning damage; effective against dragons.
            Two-handed thrust relies on cross and buries deep within a dragon's hide, and sends human foes flying."

            How to Get / Where to Find the Dragonslayer Spear

            •  Ascend +10 spear with Soul of Ornstein 

            Notes

            • Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
            • Special: 1H Strong Attack is altered to a Long Range Lightning Bolt Projectile. (Consumes 20 Durability Per Use)
            • 2H Strong Attack is altered to an advancing thrust to upwards thrust.
            • Dexterity Scaling does towards Phys. Damage, while Faith scaling contributes to Lightning damage.

            Lore

            • Ornstein was named after the composer "Leo Ornstein".
            • Ornstein reappeared as The Old Dragonslayer in DS2.
            • The armor set of Ornstein was later rediscovered at Archdragon Peak (DS3).

            Demon Titanite weapon upgrades are usually for Boss Soul Weapons that are created at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.
            Regular Reinforcement Scales 60% for  Strength,100% for  Dexterity and 100% for  Faith to increase your AR. Also, gives 100 critical bonus.
