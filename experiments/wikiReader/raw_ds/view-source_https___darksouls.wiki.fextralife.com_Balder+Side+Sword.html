            Balder Side Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

            The knights of the ancient kingdom of Balder wielded these rock-solid, long swords which are excellent for thrust attacks."
            "Balder was the home of Knight King Rendal, but the kingdom was reduced to ruins after a widespread outbreak of Undead."

             

            How to Get / Where to Find the Balder Side Sword

             

            • Rare (1%) drop from Balder Knights wielding the sword (Undead Parish and Sen's Fortress).

             

            Hints and Tips:

            • Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
            • Appreciated by players for its adaptability and effectiveness due to its good reach, balanced moveset and low Stamina cost.
            • The sword receives an A rating in Dexterity at +14/15 along the regular reinforcement path, making it excellent for Dexterity and Quality builds, and may also be buffed. The Enchanted elemental path grants an A scaling in Intelligence, making it a good option for Mages. Other elemental paths such as Lightning or Chaos are also effective for other builds, particularly when using the powerful R2 thrust attacks.
            • Has a similar move set to Longsword or Shortsword, and the same base damage as Longsword, but generally greater range at similar Stamina cost.
            • The one-handed strong attack (R2) is a thrust attack both for the first and second attacks in the combo, instead of a upswing on the second attack. The second R2 thrust is faster than the first, making it particularly dangerous if the target is staggered by the first thrust or otherwise using a slower weapon. It also tracks independently of the first thrust, enabling an experienced swordsman to punish moving targets with or without target lock.
            • Thrust attacks do significantly more damage than the slashes, and can benefit from the Leo Ring.
            • Greater range of the sword enables users to better distance themselves from targets, and reduces the likelihood of being parried provided one does not get too close. The powerful R2 thrust attacks are also very difficult to parry at range, similar to the thrust attacks of spears.
