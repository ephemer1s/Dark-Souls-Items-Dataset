            Artorias' Set is an Armor Set in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered (AotA only).

             

             

            Artorias' Set Information

            Armor of Artorias the Abysswalker, one of Gwyn's Four Knights.
            The death of the armor's owner can be surmised from the corrosive Dark of the Abyss, and the tattered azure-blue cape, once a symbol of pride and glory.

            Location

            • Purchased from Domhnall of Zena for 20,000 Souls a piece (80,000 total) after defeating Artorias the Abysswalker.

             

            Notes

            • Artorias' Set can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.

             

            Lore

            • Artorias is related to The Abyss Watchers of DS3.
            • The armor set of Artorias, named Wolf Knight Set, was later rediscovered at Untended Graves in DS3.
