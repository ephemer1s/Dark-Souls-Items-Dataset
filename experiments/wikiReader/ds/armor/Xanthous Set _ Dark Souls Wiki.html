            Xanthous Set is an Armor Set in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

             

            Xanthous Set Information

            Crown/Overcoat/Gloves/Waistcloth- A mysterious item once worn by the Xanthous King Jeremiah, the legendary exile. No one knows where it came from.
            The crown bears high-quality cloth which is quite soft to the touch, but its bright yellow color stings the eyes, and it is clearly far too big.
            Overcoat/Gloves/Waistcloth- It may be tattered, but its bright yellow color still stings the eyes.

             

             

            Location

            • Found in Painted World of Ariamis.
            • Players must have been invaded by Black Phantom King Jeremiah as well as defeated him. After meeting Crossbreed Priscilla, head towards the exit (the walkway leading out to no where it seems) and before you get close to the end of the walkway, look to the left, the armor is on a corpse in a little alcove.

             

             

            Notes

            • Xanthous Set can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.

             

             

            Bug

            • If you meet all conditions as stated above and still dont see the corpse with the armor on it; quit the game and come back. This has been proven to fix the vanishing armor set.
