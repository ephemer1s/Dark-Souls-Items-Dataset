            Crystal Straight Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

            A fully crystallized straight sword. How such a weapon was created is entirely unknown."
            "The crystallization boosts its attack, but makes the blade brittle. The sword cannot be used for long, as it cannot be repaired."

             

            How to Get / Where to Find the Crystal Straight Sword

            • Drops from Undead Crystal Soldiers (Sword) (3% chance - The Duke's Archives).
            • Purchased from Domhnall of Zena (4,000 Souls).

             

            Hints and Tips:

            • Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 10 Souls.
            • Like all Crystal weapons and armor, cannot be upgraded or repaired.
            • Not as effective as the Crystal Greatsword, since in the boss fights where it's damage is relevant, the sword and board playstyle usually isn't very helpful. However, it can be a potent throwaway weapon for dealing with regular mobs during early game, being equivalent to a Longsword +8. By the time this sword starts dropping from enemies (The Duke's Archives), it is mediocre at best, though tolerable if you feel the need to use a fast weapon.
            • Be wary when seeing this weapon in PvP, for it is rarely actually used. Instead, it is most likely a hacked version that does ~900 damage while in one hand. However it swings slightly slower than most Straight Swords.
