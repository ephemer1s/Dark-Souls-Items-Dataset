            Black Knight Greatsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

            Greatsword of the black knights who wander Lordran. Used to face chaos demons."
            "The large motion that puts the weight of the body into the attack reflects the great size of their adversaries long ago." 

            How to Get / Where to Find the Black Knight Greatsword

            • Drops from The Black Knight with Great Sword. (20% chance - Undead Parish and Kiln of the First Flame)
            • Black Knight respawn in Kiln of the First Flame.

            Hints and Tips:

            • Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
            • Rather effective as a shield breaker in pvp but if you miss you'll be left open to attacks.
            • Two handed strong attack is similar to that of the Black Knight Greataxe, but faster.
            • Special: One-handed R2 causes Knockback
            • Special: Two-handed R2 causes Knock Up
            • The Dragon Greatsword, Black Knight Greatsword and Demon Great Machete are slower than the Greatsword and the Zweihander, though they don't appear to be much slower, it is more difficult to stunlock enemies with the first three.

            Black Knight Greatsword Upgrade Table

            Unique Upgrades are performed by any Blacksmith or by using the Weapon Smithbox.
            Regular Reinforcement Scales 90% for  Strength and 5% for  Dexterity to increase your AR.
            To reach max, you need: 10,000 souls and 10  Twinkling Titanite.

This is a great weapon for Gwyn if you can parry well, as you can take him down in about three hits, or less if you use RTSR, Power Within, or both. But that last bit is not advised, because Gwyn will usually destroy you if you try him with only 20% health.
