            Light Crossbow is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

             

             

             

            Standard crossbow issued to soldiers. While bows require both hands, a crossbow is held in one, but arming each bolt takes time. "

             

            How to Get / Where to Find the Light Crossbow

            • Drops from Undead Knight Archer (2% - Sen's Fortress)
            • Drops from Balder Knight with crossbow (2% chance - Sen's Fortress and Undead Parish)
            • Drops from Undead Soldier wielding a sword (2% Chance - Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Northern Undead Asylum (Return), Firelink Shrine)
            • Video Location Timestamp 5:15

             

             

             

            Note

            • Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
            • Can not be modified towards Raw or Chaos
            • Demon's souls version: Slightly similar in apperance with the same range.

            Crystal Upgrades are performed by the Giant Blacksmith at Anor Londo. Reduces durability reduced by 15.
            Crystal Upgrades require a regular +10 weapon and to hand in the Crystal Ember.
            Crystal Reinforcement reduces weapon Durability by 15, but increases base damage. It scales 0% for Strength and 0% for Dexterity to increase your AR.
            To reach max, you need: 1000 souls, 8 titanite chunks to +4 and 1 titanite slab to +5

            Magic Upgrades are performed by Rickert of Vinheim, found in New Londo Ruins.
            Magic Upgrade +1 requires a regular +5 weapon. Magic +6 requires a Magic +5 and to hand in the Large Magic Ember
            Magic Reinforcement adds Magic Damage and an Intelligence bonus. The weapon can no longer be buffed with Magic Weapon. Scales 0% for Strength, 0% for Dexterity and 0%  Intelligence to increase your AR.
            To reach max, you need: 2,000 souls, 10 Green Titanite Shards to +5, 7 Blue Titanite Chunks to +14 and 1 Blue Titanite Slab to +15

            To reach max, you need: 2000 souls, 10 Green Titanite Shards, 7 White Titanite Chunks and 1 White Titanite Slab
