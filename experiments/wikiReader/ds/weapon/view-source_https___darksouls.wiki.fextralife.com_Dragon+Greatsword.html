            585 100
            - 44
            - 400
            - 24.0
            Requirements & Bonus
            50 10 – –
            Weapon Type Ultra Greatsword
            Attack Type Regular
            Enchantable No
            Special Yes

            *When infused with Magic: 150 physical dmg/162 magic dmg, with Fire: 192 physical dmg/192 Fire dmg, with Lightning: 200 physical dmg/200 Lightning dmg
             

            Dragon Greatsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

            This sword, one of the rare dragon weapons, came from the tail of the stone dragon of Ash Lake, descendant of the ancient dragons."
            "Its great mystical power will be unleashed when wielded with two hands."

            How to Get / Where to Find the Dragon Greatsword

            • Obtained by cutting off the Everlasting Dragon's Tail in Ash Lake.
            • Note: This will not make him hostile or break the Covenant. Tail does grow back, but cutting it second time doesn't give you the weapon anymore. Ancient Stone Dragon does not become hostile if you do so, or attack it in any other way.
            • Video Location Timestamp 7:15

             

             

            Hints and Tips:

            • Give to Frampt to receive 5000 souls.
            • Special: Gives +20 Magic and +20 Fire resistance.
            • Special: Two-handed R2 shoots a shock wave in a straight line along the ground, and will even travel up walls. Using this ability reduces the weapon durability by 50 points per use.
            • Only 34 Strength is required to use it if held in both hands.
            • The Dragon Greatsword, Black Knight Greatsword and Demon Great Machete are slower than the Greatsword and the Zweihander. Though they don't appear to be much slower, it is more difficult to stunlock enemies with the first three.

             

             

            Lore

            • The Dragon Greatsword is part of a theme that connects the Everlasting Dragons to stone.

                The more I look at this thing, the more I realise it's not worth the trouble of using it.

                It require 50 strenght to be used for 585 base dammages at +5 but with no strenght scaling whatsoever meaning no bonus dammages based on strenght. Not even a low bonus but no bonus at all.

                The Demon Great Machete in comparison is much lighter (18 for the machete, 24 for this), only require 40 STR to be used and even at 40 strenght and "only" 332 of base dammages the machete will easily go above 600 AR thanks to it's A scaling in strenght thus far outclassing this Dragon Greatsword.

                Even the Black Knight Greatsword with 330 base dammages and "only" a B in Strenght is far superior in AR and also has a natural dammage bonus to demon's (and is even lighter than the Machete) and-like all black knight weapons, deal the most poise dammages out of any other weapon of their respective weapon class meaning it's next to impossible to just eat it's attack and poise through it.

                Sure, it outclass the other UGS, the Zweihander and the greatsword in term of pure physical dammages that is true HOWEVER unlike the Zweihander or the Greatsword, this thing is still worthless because these two are accessible early game, demand far less stat investment and can be ascended to be put to other uses such as lightning or chaos thus making them more versatile and this versatility allow them to outclass the Dragon Greatsword with the correct build like the famous Chaos Zweihander with high humanity level for massive fire dammages.

                So long story short, if you want raw physical dammages, the Demon Great Machete and the Black Knight Greatsword will outclass any other Ultra Greatswords while the Zweihander and the Greatsword are not as strong physically (yes) but have the advantage of being able to change scaling based on what ascension you put on them making them very versatile and allowing them to make very high dammages regardless.

                So what is the point of this Dragon Greatsword compared to the other members of it's weapon class ? It cannot deal elemental dammages, require too much strenght for no scaling in strenght whatsoever, is stupidly heavy and is outclassed by two other UGS with far lower stat requirements so really why use this weapon ?

                The weapon art ? This thing that have a slow activation time, eat up 50 points of the weapon's durability and can't even reach that far unless we're talking about very specific situations ? Long story short, trash Ultra Greatsword, I don't recommend you use it unless you wanna troll or something...
