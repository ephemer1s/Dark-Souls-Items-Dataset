            Gargoyle's Halberd is a Weapon in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered.

             

             

             

            Halberd of the gargoyle guarding the Bell of Awakening in the Undead Church."
            "Perfectly standard bronze halberd without any special power."

             

             

             

            How to Get / Where to Find the Gargoyle's Halberd

            • Random drop from either the Bell Gargoyles or the two Anor Londo Gargoyles. Can be farmed for by killing only one Bell Gargoyle and quitting the game/dying.
            • A Gargoyle is located at Undead Parish. Anor Londo has two more Gargoyles.
            • Video Location Timestamp 2:55




            Notes

            • Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
            • Special: Increases Bleeding, Poison and Toxin resistance by 25%. (inventory screen = +60/+20, but equipped = 25%)
            • Has a moveset similar to the Scythe, instead of the normal Halberd.
