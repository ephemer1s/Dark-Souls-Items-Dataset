            Eastern Armor Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Distinctive armor made in an Eastern land.

            The exquisite craftmanship and artistic design made these prized pieces in the collection of any nobleman.

            Offers excellent damage absorption, particularly from slashing attacks from katanas, which are commonly encountered threats in battles fought in the East.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Bought from Shrine Handmaid in Firelink Shrine for 23,000 souls after giving her the Easterner's Ashes found in Irithyll of the Boreal Valley, beneath the Silver Knights.

            Notes and Tips

            • The armor once belonged to Shiva of the East, a character from the original Dark Souls.
