            Outrider Knight Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Armor of an Irithyll outrider knight. Enveloped in a dimly cool air.

            The knights were given the eyes of the Pontiff, but the eyes transformed them into savage, raving warriors who only knew how to serve as mindless guards.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by a Boreal Outrider Knight behind an illusory wall near the top of the Grand Archives.
            • Video Location (Fextralife video starting at 19m 24s)




            Notes and Tips

            • Very high frost resistance. Especially useful when fighting enemies that apply Frostbite like Outrider Knights and Vordt.
