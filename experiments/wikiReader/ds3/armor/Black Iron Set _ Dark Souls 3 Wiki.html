            Black Iron Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Armor made of black iron, from the set of armor for which Knightslayer Tsorig was infamously known.

            Offers extensive and particularly effective protection from fire.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Sold by Shrine Handmaid in Firelink Shrine for 21,500 souls after defeating Knight Slayer Tsorig in Smouldering Lake.





            Notes and Tips

            • A direct reference to the first Dark Souls, where it is worn by an NPC named Black Iron Tarkus.
            • Has excellent slash and fire defense.
            • Grants the second highest fire resistance for an armor set when all pieces are worn, surpassed only by Havel's Set.

                                                                              For its weight, this armor has some really good damage reduction and (((poise))). Its poise is just about enough to make you tank through a single hit of the heaviest weapons without being staggered (provided you are mid-swing yourself) while its weight (while using Havel's ring and ring of favor) is just about under what is needed to use the heaviest weapons in the game or a greatshield+lighter 2hander combo without exceeding 100% equip load or spending points in VIT.


                                                                              If you have access to the best rings in the game, they can make this armor a great choice for a quality build either at SL 120 or for a twink, and allow you to get this great protection without investing in anything other than damage stats or HP. You can't really do this with Havel's or Smough's because they are far too heavy, and even Gundyr's will force you to limit yourself to less-than-heaviest weapons available, while any of these will be offering only marginal increases in protection (~1-2%) or a technically irrelevant increment in poise (such an increment that would not put you over the next treshold required to tank another hit of anything heavier than a 1H sword). However, since you already would be wearing such rings, each point of VIT would be more efficient, so it's not of much importance for high soul level builds, but dropping these items on a twink would allow you to use Ledo's alongside this armor at SL 26 for a depraved, which is an insane combination of protection and offensive capability at such a low level.
