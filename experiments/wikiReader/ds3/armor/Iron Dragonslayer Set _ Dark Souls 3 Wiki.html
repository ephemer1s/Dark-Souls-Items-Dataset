            Iron Dragonslayer Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3. It was added in The Ringed City DLC.




            Stark melted iron body armor of the ancient Dragonslayer Armour.

            The Armour, defeated by the Champion of Ash in Lothric, was left ages to rust, until it slipped into an abyssal swamp, where it was possessed once again by the memory of the hunt. 





            Location/Where to Find

            • The Ringed City: Drops from the Dragonslayer Armour in the swamp outside of the Ringed City Streets bonfire.





            Notes and Tips

            • The helmet has very prominent teeth that form a sort of grin, despite having no visible eyeholes or anything else that would make it possible for a human to wear.
