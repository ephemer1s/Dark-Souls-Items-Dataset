            Morne's Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Unusual helm bestowed upon knights of Carim. (Helmet.)

            Black (armor/gauntlets/leggings) bestowed upon knights of Carim.

            Modeled on Morne, the Archbishop's apostle, cast from a unique mineral resembling stone.

            A Carim knight will dedicate an entire career to attending a single maiden, just as Morne once served one goddess alone.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Bought from Shrine Handmaid in Firelink Shrine for 23,000 souls after either killing Eygon of Carim or defeating Dragonslayer Armour.




            Notes and Tips

            • The set can be obtained early by getting Eygon to fall of the cliff.
            • Alternatively, lure the two Undead Manservants from the previous section past the bridge over the ravine and bait attacks from them to hit him, killing him without incurring Sin.
