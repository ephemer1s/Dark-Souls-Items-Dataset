            Slave Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3. It is part of the Ashes of Ariandel DLC.




            Armor Issued to slave knights. The fine craftsmanship made this a symbol of honor.

            Long ago, only the Undead served as slave knights, warriors used as fodder in the bleakest of battles.

            They grew decrepit, their skin charred black and their bones twisted. Eventually they went outright mad, but were never relieved from duty.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Located in the house directly across the bridge from the Corvian Settlement Bonfire
              • From the bonfire, cross the bridge and go up the hill. Drop right, past the Corvian Knight, onto the ridge path. Follow this route until you climb a ladder. Enter the tower on the rooftop, but instead of climbing the ladder, drop between the floorboards into the building. Beware the ambush!




            Notes and Tips

            • The armor set of the NPC Slave Knight Gael.
            • The Slave Set has some of the best absorption relative to its weight,
              and is therefore great for builds with low to medium vitality.
