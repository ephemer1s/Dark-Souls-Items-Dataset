            Gundyr's Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Ancient armor of a set of cast iron armor, belonging to Champion Gundyr. Modeled after a former king.

            Gundyr, or the Belated Champion, was bested by an unknown warrior. He then became sheath to a coiled sword in the hopes that someday, the first flame would be linked once more.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Bought from the Shrine Handmaid in Firelink Shrine for 36,000 souls after defeating Champion Gundyr.




            Notes and Tips

            • The former king mentioned in the armor description could be Old King Doran from Demon's Souls, the helmet being reminiscent of Doran's own helmet and the title of Doran itself.
            • Leaks from quality assurance testers of the alpha version suggest that Iudex Gundyr was originally going to play the role of Oceiros, using that name, in The Consumed King's Garden, with Oceiroso appearing in a cut area called "God's Grave". Gundyr would still be a character, but look and play like Yhorm the Giant - (credit darksouls3theorist and Lance McDonald).
