            Vilhelm's Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.  It is part of the Ashes of Ariandel DLC.




            The black (piece name) of Vilhelm, Knight of Londor, and a nice fit for any lean Hollow.

            This hollow knight, who served the three sisters who founded the Sable Church, was particularly loyal to the eldest, and was known as a cold-blooded hangman.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Painted World of Ariandel: Sir Vilhelm, Knight of Londor's armor. His armor can be found in the crypt filled with bugs where you turn the crank handle to enable the Sister Friede boss fight.





            Notes and Tips

            • The ID of Vilhelm's Set once belonged to the Burial Knight Set, an armor set that was cut from the game.

                                                                                                                                          *SPOILERS*

                                                                                                                                          For all those curious, Vilhelm doesn't drop his armor when killed, it's on a corpse. Once you reach the Snowy Mountainpass Bonfire (Past the Corian Village), it's in a hidden location through an illusiory wall past an army of those fly *****ers. The area is on a ridge past two Milwood knights guarding a cave. Just follow the paths rightward and you should find them with little difficulty. In the cave behind them, there's a square room with some fly people in it, and the illusory wall is in the far left corner. There are some messages there already, so it shouldn't be too hard to find. After that, follow the stairs to the nasty ass hive place, and it's the farthest item to the right.
