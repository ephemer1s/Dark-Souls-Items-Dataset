            Wolf Knight Armor is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.


            Helm of a knight tainted by the dark of the Abyss. The twilight blue tassel is damp, and will ever remain so.

            A vanquished knight left behind only wolf's blood, and legacy of duty. The Undead Legion of Farron was formed to bear his torch, and the armor of these Abyss Watchers suggests their own eventual end.

            Location/Where to Find

            • Buy from Shrine Handmaid in Untended Graves for 46,000 Souls.

            Lore

            • Artorias is tied to The Abyss Watchers of DS3.

            Notes and Tips

            • The player MUST kill the Abyss Watchers before this set becomes available.
            • This the same armor worn by Artorias the Abysswalker in the first Dark Souls.
