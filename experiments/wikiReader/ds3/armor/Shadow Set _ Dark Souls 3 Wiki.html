            Shadow Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Black cloth (Piece Name) worn by spooks from an Eastern land. Sacrfices defense for greater mobility.

            The late King Oceiros was obsessed by dragons, to the extent that he would later be known as the Consumed King. Countless assassins were sent to end his reign, but none returned.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Found amongst the Rotten Slugs in the poisonous lake in the Consumed King's Garden (after the beginning elevator go around the staircase in front of you)





            Notes and Tips

            • On female players, the chest/Gloves together will not cover the entire arm
