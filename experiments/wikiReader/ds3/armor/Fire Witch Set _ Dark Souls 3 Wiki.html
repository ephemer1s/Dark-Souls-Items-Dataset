            Fire Witch Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Armor of witches who bore the Profaned Flame, now harrowed spirits of Irithyll.

            The witches who lead the Pontiff's Knights were originally ordained as holy knights. It was not long however, before their hearts were swallowed by the Profaned Flame.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Fire Witches in Irithyll of the Boreal Valley.





            Notes and Tips

            • Can be easily farmed in NG, with less than 300 AR : from the Church of Yorshka bonfire, exit through the double doors, make a left along the ledge, follow the path until you see a broken railing. You can drop here right onto a Fire Witch (should take at least half its HP away) and finish it before it can even move. To go back to the bonfire, climb the stairs in front of the broken railing and go back the way you came. Rinse and repeat.

            Trivia

            • Has an inspired look from the boss Fume Knight from Dark Souls 2 DLC.
