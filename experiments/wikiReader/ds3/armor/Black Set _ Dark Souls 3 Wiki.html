            Black Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Attire of the three mentors of the Sable Church of Londor...
            ..this billed mask belongs to Yuria, the second eldest. (Helm)
            ..this pitch-black dress resembles a mourning dress. (Dress)
            ..these black leggings/gauntlets are hidden by the dress. (Leggings & gauntlets)

            These maidens of a Primordial Serpent were renowned as founders of the Sable Church, which offered salvation for Hollows. (Helm & Dress)

            It is apparent that the women in black were highly skilled fencers, capable of founding the Sable Church between just the three of them. (Leggings & Gauntlets)





            Location/Where to Find

            • Sold by the Shrine Handmaid after you have killed Yuria and hand in her ashes (You will need to reload the area) for 18000 souls, or you can complete Yuria's questline to receive her Darkdrift and armor set at her location in Firelink Shrine. (You need to summon her to Soul of Cinder boss fight and she must survive)





            Notes and Tips

            • Bears resemblance to the Cainhurst armor set from Bloodborne.
