            Antiquated Plain Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3 The Ringed City DLC.

             

            Ordinary garb from an ancient land of sorcery. The gold embellishments betray a faint residue of magic, but this clothing was never intended for battle.

            When a mission visited the Ringed City long ago, one of its younger missionaries elected to stay behind. It was he who became the last recorded Spear of the Church.

            Location/Where to Find

            • Antiquated Plain Garb and Violet Wrappings can be found right before the Darkeater Midir boss fight. From the Ringed Inner Wall Bonfire take the shortcut path on the left and take the lift down. On the left wall of the lift about halfway down is a side passage and roll onto it as you go. Take a left until you come to a room with Silver Knight Statues, and hit the right statue on the wall, that of a Silver Knight missing its blade, to reveal an illusory wall. Climb the ladder down and you will see the set in the center of the room, in front of a fog gate.
            • The Loincloth can be found in the swamp in The Dreg Heap surrounded by Poisonhorn Bugs

             

            Notes and Tips

            • This set is worn by Halflight, Spear of the Church
