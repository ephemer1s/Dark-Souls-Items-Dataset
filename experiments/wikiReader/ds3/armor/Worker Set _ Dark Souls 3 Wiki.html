            Worker Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




             

            (Piece Name) worn by inhabitants of the undead Settlement.

            Official attire for the dissection and burial of undead. Naturally the ceremonial significance of such work is long forgotten,

            Indeed, no one could continue to entertain such horrors.





            Location/Where to find

            • Dropped by Peasant Hollows in Undead Settlement
            • Best area to farm is where the giant throw arrows as you can sit back and reap the rewards.





            Notes and Tips

            • Has high Poison resistance.
            • There are some Peasant Hollow NPCs that wears an variant Worker Garb that has a Dark Red Tint on the upper cloth. However, there's no mention that an unused version was made for the player as well in the cut content.
