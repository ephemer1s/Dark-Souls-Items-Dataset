            Dragonscale Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            (Piece Name) of a nameless king who was ally to the ancient dragons. 

            This golden crown, buried amidst long strands of bristling ash, is said to closely resemble that of the First Lord. (Helm)

            Dragon scales are razor-sharp and cannot be burned. (Chest)





            Location/Where to Find

            • Buy from Shrine Handmaid for 36,000 souls after killing The Nameless King.




            Notes and Tips

            • The Dragonscale Set is aesthetically very similar to the Great Lord's set, the armor made purchaseable from Domhnall of Zena after defeating Gwyn, Lord of Cinder in Dark Souls. Noticeable differences are the choice in weapon and added armor and cloth draped over the robes.
            • The lightning defense is ironically low considering that the armor belongs to the son of Gwyn and the boss that wears this set is resistant to lightning. Though it's possible the reason for this could be because pieces of the armor are made of dragon scales, and the dragons are weak to lightning.
            • For some reason, wearing the chest piece at the same time as the Cleric Trousers may result in your character having a large hole in their model where their waist should be
