            Ringed Knight Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3. It was added in The Ringed City DLC.




            Malformed black armor of the Ringed Knights. The armor of early men was forged in the Abyss, and betrays a smidgen of life.

            For this reason the gods cast a seal of fire upon such armor, and those who possessed them.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Can drop from Ringed Knights





            Notes and Tips

            • It's recommended to use a Covetous Gold Serpent Ring or any of the item discorvery boosting gear when farming for armor peices, as they appear to have a low drop rate.
            • Each piece can be sold to Shrine Handmaid for 600 souls.
