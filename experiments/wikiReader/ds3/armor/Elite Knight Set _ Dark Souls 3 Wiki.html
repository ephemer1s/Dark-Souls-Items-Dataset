            Elite Knight Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Armor said to have been given to elite knights of fallen Astora.

            The mere mention of Astora invoked wistful pangs, and perhaps it was such a dream that drew Anri to this faraway home known only by name.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Buy from the Shrine Handmaid in Firelink Shrine after completing Anri of Astora's questline (or killing Anri) for 13500 souls.



            Notes and Tips

            • This set is identical to its counterparts in Dark Souls 1 and 2, and is popularly considered to be the most iconic set from the original Dark Souls
            • The Japanese reads: "Armor given to senior knights in the fallen Astora. The name of Astora caused nostalgia/homesickness, and that motivated Anri's desires. It is a distant homeland of which nothing is known but the name."
