            Black Witch Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3. It was added in The Ringed City DLC.




            The purple (armor piece) of the witch Zullie, who intended to seduce Alva the Wayfarer, but eventually became his closest supporter, spending her entire life with him.

            It is said that Zullie the witch, who was never loved, nor loved another, experienced all manner of misfortune, and yet in the end, found her purpose in life.





            Location/Where to Find

            • The Ringed City: Located on a corpse near Shira. Head across the bridge over the steps near Shira and go left through a couple of courtyards to find it. It is guarded by Dark Spirit Alva, Seeker of the Spurned.
            • The veil is found in a corner guarded by a Ringed Knight in the Ringed City Streets.





            Notes and Tips

            • The leggings will differ depending on gender: pants for males and a mini skirt with high heels for females.
            • Has very good elemental and curse resistance.
