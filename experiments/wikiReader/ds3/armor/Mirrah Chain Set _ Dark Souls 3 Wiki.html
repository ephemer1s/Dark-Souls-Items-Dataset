            Mirrah Chain Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Formal attire of the honorable knights of Mirrah, featuring their heraldry, a stag set against a blue field.

            Oddly, it was a dishonorable deserter who wore this attire most religiously.





            Location/Where to Find

            • On the Irithyll of the Boreal Valley bridge into the city. Can only be found on the bridge after, being invaded by Creighton the Wanderer outside the Church of Yorshka bonfire. (He will only invade if you beat him with Sirris of the Sunless Realms in her quest)





            Notes and Tips

            • Has low lightning defense but has decent slash absorption.
            • The set of the similarly named NPC Creighton the Wanderer in Dark Souls II.

If you defeat Aldrich before helping Sirris against Creighton, her questline skips to the next step and you can't defeat Creighton anymore.After you defeat Creighton near the Church, go back to the bridge and the armor will be on a corpse.
