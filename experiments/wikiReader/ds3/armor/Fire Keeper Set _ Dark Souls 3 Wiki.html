            Fire Keeper Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Robe, Manchettes and Skirt worn by the guardian of the shrine.

            The Fire Keepers were robbed of light, to better serve as vessels for souls. Only those who cherish the writhing, searing darkness were given the keeper's black attire.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Located in the Bell Tower of Firelink Shrine, unlocked with the Tower Key.
            • The tower entrance is found upstairs and outside, reached by climbing the left hand staircase when you are about to exit the shrine. Open the tower door and climb the spiral staircase, then cross the bridge and enter the large tower at the other end. Drop down to the shiny item on the left side, when facing the elevator.
            • Video Location (Fextralife Video starting at 1h 55m 15s)




            Notes and Tips

            • in order to safely reach the platform where the set lies, just run towards the corner and let yourself fall into a plunging attack (R1 while falling) with a fist or a very short weapon. This will let you land safely on the platform. Using a long weapon like an greatsword or ultra weapon will result in pushing you too far from the wall an to drop dead. On the platfrom, collect the set and chung an estus before resuming the fall towards the estus ring. Like so: Video demonstration
