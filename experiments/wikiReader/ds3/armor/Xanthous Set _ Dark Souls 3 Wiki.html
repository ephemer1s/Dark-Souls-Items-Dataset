            Xanthous Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.

             

            Crown made in imitation of a divine creature of Oolacile, land of ancient, golden sorceries.

            Xanthous clothing is the mark of a researcher of lost sorceries, and the oversized crown is emblematic of their work. Such a curious pursuit is surely nothing to be ashamed of. (Crown)

             

            Yellowish overcoat covering a band of brass medallions.

            Xanthous clothing is the mark of a researcher of lost sorceries, and the number of medallions denotes their success. A point of pride for the researchers, even if its signficance is lost on others. (Overcoat)

             

            Yellowish gloves.

            Xanthous clothing is the mark of a researcher of lost sorceries, and it is said that with golden sorceries, one could manipulate light. (Gloves)

             

            Yellowish trousers.

            Xanthous clothing is the mark of a researcher of lost sorceries, and it is said that with golden sorceries, one could manipulate light. (Trousers)





            Location/Where to Find

            • The crown is dropped by Yellowfinger Heysel after killing her invading phantom in either Road of Sacrifices  or Farron Keep or can be gained by killing the Man-grub that spawns in Rosaria's Bedchamber after both Yhorm and Aldrich are defeated.  Defeating The Abyss Watchers boss will cause Yellowfinger Heysel to no longer invade, so the first method must be done before beating the boss. The rest of the armor set can be bought from the Shrine Handmaid in Firelink Shrine for 24,000 souls after giving her Xanthous Ashes (found in Irithyll Dungeon in the room with lots of Jailers).





            Notes and Tips

            • The set is visually drastically different compared to its appearance in Dark Souls 1 and 2. The crown is now shaped like a mushroom and is slightly reminiscent of the Monk's Head Collar from Demon's Souls. The rest of the set also now leaves the wearer fully clothed.  Much of the set is stained with blood.
