            Lapp's Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3. It was added in The Ringed City DLC.




            Steel armor of the amnesiac Lapp.

            This plate-armor body, that covers the torso cleanly without even slight gaps, provides heavy defense.

            Quite fit to conceal a hollowed body, and indeed one's very identity.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Complete the Lapp Questline.
            • Kill Lapp and purchase the set from Shrine Handmaid for 31,000 souls.
            • He will reside in the cave past where you met Midir on the Bridge. Make a right and you'll find Lapp squatting. Talk to him and you will get a cutscene. To get his armor set you must work your way back to where you receive the cutscene.




            Notes and Tips

            • It is recommended that you do the questline instead of killing him to save souls. It is a very straightforward quest that sheds some light on the game's long building lore.
            • This set was originally cut from the base game and was previously named the Heavy Knight's Set (shown below). It also had a more silver color than Lapp's Set.
            • The overall design of Lapp's Set is based of the Stechzeug, a suit of armor for Jousting.
