            Grave Warden Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Rotting, tattered skirt, robe, hood and wrap. 
            Attire of grave wardens at the Cathedral of the Deep.

            Grave wardens were tasked with disposing of the ever rising corpses that plagued the cathedral. Their clothes are utterly putrid, drenched in the blood and mucilage of their undertaking.





            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by the Cathedral Grave Wardens at Cathedral of the Deep and Untended Graves.





            Notes and Tips

            • Farming tips:
              • The fastest and easiest farming spot for this set is the Untended Graves first bonfire.
                • Exit the passage and go straight, towards the first Warden, which is slightly beyond the arch. He will usually aggro when you approach the Lordvessel-like bowl, so using a Rusted Coin when you get close to it is the optimal time.
                • Killing this one quickly while luring him closer to the bowl will ensure that the second one, patrolling behind the arch, will not aggro. After which you can go through the arch and aggro that one.
                • The second Warden will partrol towards the dogs on the right side, but they do not aggro as easily, so luring him out or simply being quick with the first one is best.
              • An alternate and much earlier area to farm them is in the Cathedral of the Deep, after opening the door to the left of the Cleansing Chapel bonfire when facing the main door.
                • Go through this left door and up the elevator. To the right, there is a room with 3 Deacons in it. Go past them, through the hall with a slimey foe in it, then out the large doors to the right, across from the giant.
                • There will be 3 enemies which will self immolate. After dealing with them, go down the stairs to the right when facing out the large doors. There you will find 2 Grave Wardens.
                • If you run past the self-immolating enemies slowly enough, you can use them to kill the first Grave Warden (and themselves).
