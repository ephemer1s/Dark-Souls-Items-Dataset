            Firelink Set is an Armor in Dark Souls 3.




            Armor of the Soul of Cinder, a deific manifestation of the Lords of Cinder, who linked the First Flame.

            It resembles a knight's armor, but bears hideous burns and contortions...

            ..A mishappen crown can be seen upon its rear. (Helm) /
            ..taking the shape of a deathly ribcage. (Chest)

            It exists as a symbol of the great Lords and the noble act of linking the fire, though it is no more than an empty husk. (Chest, Gauntlets, Leggings)





            Location/Where to Find

            • Buy from Shrine Handmaid in Firelink Shrine for 56000 souls after defeating Soul of Cinder or after defeating the boss as a phantom in another player's world. 
            • You do not need to enter New Game Plus to be able to buy it, but it will still be available.

            Trivia

            • Much like the Elite Knight Set and the Faraam Set from the previous games, this armor set is considered to be the flagship armor of Dark Souls 3, as it appears in the box art, and is featured as the red knight figurine of the collector's edition and in the Funko Pop line.
