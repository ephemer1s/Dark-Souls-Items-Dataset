            Wolnir's holy Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A holy sword eroded by the Abyss. When Wolnir fell to the Abyss, he was gripped by a fear of true darkness, and pleaded to the gods for the first time.

            This holy sword, together with three armlets stripped from the corpses of clerics, gave him some semblance of comfort.

            Skill: Wrath of the Gods
            Thrust weapon into earth to emit powerful shockwave. The wrath of those swallowed by the Abyss is a thing to be wary of indeed.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Buffed or Infused.
            • Reinforces with Titanite Scale.
            • Has the same moveset as the Bastard Sword, with the exception of the Weapon Art.
            • The initial thrust of the Weapon Art has a hitbox. But it does not true-combo into the Blast of the WA. 
            • Received Faith scaling and altered Strength and Dexterity scaling in the 1.09 regulation patch.
            • Wolnir's Holy Sword has a range of 35.3243xl on the 1h R1 and 37.7386xl on the 2h R1.

                                                                        This is a PVE weapon, pretty much end of discussion. In PVP, it has too many weakpoints. No hyper armor during the WA, dual scaling stats, and a predictable moveset. You can still win with skill, but you're going to be severely handicapped. As for PVE though, this sword is great for Paladin-type characters. If you start as a Knight, you only need one point of DEX extra to use it (and FTH, but you're gonna level that anyways.) Even at 10 Attunement, you have enough FP for three WAs. You can basically focus on VIG, VIT, STR, and FTH until higher levels. 18 Attunement is enough Great Heal and Sacred Oath, and enough FP to cast them twice or have four WAs. Learning how to use the WA properly is key. After a backstab, you will connect with the WA 99% with no punishment, which leaves the enemy prone for a second time which you can follow with a Charged R2. Learn to connect with the first hit of the WA as well, it deals decent damage and can break poise. And watch your FP, not having enough will perform the LONG animation for no damage.
