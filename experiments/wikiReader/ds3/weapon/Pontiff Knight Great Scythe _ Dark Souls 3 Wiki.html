            Pontiff Knight Great Scythe is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Great scythe wielded by the Pontiff's knights, frigid spirits that linger in Irithyll.

            The large blade appears to be eaten away by insects, making it lightweight but also brittle.

            Skill: Frost
            Create a bone-chilling frost with each swing of the great scythe that causes a temporary frost effect.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Despite the S scaling in DEX, it is weaker than a Sharp Great Corvian Scythe  and is only slightly stronger than a Sharp Great Scythe with 60 DEX and 20 STR.
            • Prior to regulation 1.33, the weapon stats did not show that the weapon inflicted Bleed. This left many players wondering whether the scythe dealing Bleed damage was a glitch or not.
            • Has a "Sweet Spot" meaning the weapon loses 10% of its damage if it hits with the pole instead of the blade. 

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by the Pontiff Knight wielding this weapon in front of the Pontiff Sulyvahn boss room.
