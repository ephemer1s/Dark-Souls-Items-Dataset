            Crystal Sage's Rapier is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Thrusting Sword with tiny crystals scattered across its blade, used by the Crystal Sages for self-defence.

            The crystals boost the magic damage inflicted by the sword, and the item discovery of its wielder, fruit of the lifetime of research conducted by the sages.

            Skill: Stance.
            From stance, use normal attack to back step and execute a surprise attack, or a strong attack for consecutive thrusting.



            Notes & Tips:

            • Infusable: NO
            • Buffable: NO
            • Upgraded with Titanite Scales.
            • Increases Item Discovery by 50 while equipped in active slot (stat requirements are not needed). Two-handing a weapon in the other hand will not cancel the effect. Wielding two will provide 100 Item Discovery.
              • Stacks with the Symbol of Avarice, Covetous Gold Serpent Ring, and either a Rusted Coin or a Rusted Gold Coin.
            • Is the longest Thrusting Sword in the game.
            • Can't be used to parry in off-hand weapon slot.

             

            Scaling & Softcaps:

            • 1 Handed Stats, STR/DEX/INT 
              • 13/18/7 = 271 AR = Base Stats & STR/DEX softcaps
              • 40/18/7 = 284 AR
              • 13/40/7 = 285 AR
              • 40/40/7 = 298 AR
              • 13/18/40 = 378 AR = INT softcap
              • 13/18/60 = 394 AR = Caster Stats
              • 40/40/40 = 404 AR
            • After softcaps it gets less than 2 AR per level
            • Pure INT weapon
              • DEX & STR softcap right at base requirements
              • INT scales well to 40 

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Transposed from Soul of a Crystal Sage.
