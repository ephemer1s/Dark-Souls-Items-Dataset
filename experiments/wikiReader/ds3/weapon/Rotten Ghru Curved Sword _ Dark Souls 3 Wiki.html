            Rotten Ghru Curved Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A crude, half-rotten curved sword.  

            Choice weapon of the blunt-horned Ghrus, descendants of the acolytes of Farron Keep. The rancid blade is drenched in rotten waste, making it acutely poisonous.

            Skill: Spin Slash
            Slice into foes with a large spinning motion, and continue spinning to transition into strong attack.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Reinforced using Titanite.
            • Can be used to parry attacks when equipped in the left hand.



             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Ghru in Farron Keep swamp and in Smouldering Lake demon ruins.
