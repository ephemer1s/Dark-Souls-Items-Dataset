            Lothric's Holy Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Prince Lothric's straight sword, blessed by Emma with potent magic. 

            Young Lothric was meant to be a champion, and was expected to wield this platinum sword, but some things will remain distant dreams forever.

            Skill: Sacred Lothric Light
            Assume stance to imbue sword with sacred light, and use strong attack to release light  together with a great thrust of the sword.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite Scale.
            • Can be combined with Lorian's Greatsword to make the Twin Princes' Greatsword.
            • Was given minimal Faith scaling in Regulation 1.09.
            • Despite its name, this weapon does not deal Holy damage and will not prevent skeletons from re-forming.
            • This is one of seven straight sword weapons with a different or unique weapon skill (the others being the Dark Sword, Gotthard Twinswords, Sunlight Straight Sword, Valorheart, Cleric's Candlestick and the Ringed Knight Straight Sword); all the other straight swords have the skill Stance.
            • Contrary to popular belief, Lothric's Holy Sword is the second longest vanilla straight sword in the game, being slightly longer than the Dark Sword and slightly shorter than Gotthard Twinswords with a range of 35.8006xl on the 1h R1 and 35.4307xl on the 2h R1.
            • Even on a dedicated build, the weapon is easily outclassed in damage by other swords, its only benefit is the Weapon Art.

            Softcaps & Scaling:

            • 10/18/14 = 251 AR = Base Stats
            • 26/18/14 = 300 AR = Str Softcap
            • 10/40/14 = 313 AR = Dex Softcap
            • 26/40/14 = 363 AR = Combined Softcaps

            Location/Where to Find:

            • Soul Transposition of Soul of the Twin Princes
