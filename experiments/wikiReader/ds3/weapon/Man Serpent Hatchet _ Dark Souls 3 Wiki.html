            Man Serpent Hatchet is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Hatchet of the serpent men guarding Archdragon Peak. Has a unique, curved blade that can sneak past shields.

            Skill: Warcry
            Let out a spirited warcry that temporarily boosts attack, and pierce through shields to inflict direct damage.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Has the same scaling, weight, and damage as the Battle Axe. However, it has more range than the Battle Axe and can penetrate shields like the Shotel and the Carthus Shotel.
            • Tied with the Butcher Knife for the most range out of all the axes.

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Drops from Serpent Men wielding it in Archdragon Peak.

             

             

            Lore

            • Man-Serpents are the descendants of dragons, and were likely inspired by the Berserk manga (read more).
