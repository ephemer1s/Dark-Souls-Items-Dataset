            Anri's Straight Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Sword precious to Anri, another Unkindled. The dullest type of blade found in the ruined land of Astora.

            Only, it was once the sword of an earnest noble figure, and its attacks are boosted by that elusive, essential property unique to humans: luck.

            Skill: Stance
            While in stance, use normal attack to break a foe's guard from below, and strong attack to slash upwards with a forward lunge.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Infusable: NO
            • Buffable: YES
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • All weapons have motion values assigned to each attack, which are modifiers to AR that are associated with the attack being used.
              • The Barbed Straight Sword has a motion value of 200 on backstab, 250 on riposte, and 60+225 on Hornet Ring riposte. The motion value of a backstab with a straight sword does not change with Hornet Ring.
            • Anri's Straight Sword has a range of 34.157xl on the 1h R1 33.5592xl on the 2h R1.
            • Anri's Straight Sword has thrusting R2 attacks.
            • Naturally possesses HP regen and Faith scaling for physical damage.
              • Does 10% bonus damage vs. reanimated. This weapon, like Blessed Gem weapons and the Blessed Weapon spell, will prevent skeletons from respawning.
              • Regen effect is 2 HP / tick. The tick is 2 seconds long at +5.
              • Equipping the sword will instantly tick the health regen, rapidly equipping and unequipping the sword will offer faster health regeneration.
            • Naturally possesses approximately A scaling in Luck.
              • This weapon has just under 400 AR at the minimum requirements and 40 Luck.
              • The weapon is used in some Speedruns for its decent damage and health regeneration exploit.

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Drops from Anri of Astora if killed or if you complete his/her quest line. If you complete Anri's questline, the sword will be given to you by Ludleth of Courland at Firelink Shrine. Note that killing Anri will disrupt Yuria's quest line and cause her to leave.
            • If you married Anri in the Darkmoon tomb, it will be in the same location as the cutscene.
