            Murakumo is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            The curved greatsword of Alva, Seeker of the Spurned, specially forged in an eastern land. Sharp but heavy, this sword requires extreme strength and dexterity to wield.

            The search for the spurned had no end, and so the wayfaring knight warmed to a most deformed weapon.

            Skill: Spin Slash
            Slice into foes with a large spinning motion, and continue spinning to transition into a strong attack.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Has A scaling in INT/FTH with Dark or Chaos infusions, and S when Sharp-infused.
            • The longest Curved Greatsword.
            • A Sharp Murakumo with 40/40 STR/DEX has 544 AR, while a Refined one has 527 AR. 

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Dark Spirit Alva, Seeker of the Spurned just before Irithyll Dungeon bonfire.

                                                                                                                                                                  So interestingly it appears that after this new dex scaling buff, a Sharp +10 weapon with S scaling will actually get VERY significant scaling PAST 60 points in dex. Even up to around 75-80 dex you still get 2-3 damage per point at times! This odd scaling doesn't start till after 60 though for some reason.

                                                                                                                                                                  Sharp +10 @ 40 Dex = 528 Damage
                                                                                                                                                                  @ 50 Dex = 547 Damage (+19)
                                                                                                                                                                  @ 60 Dex = 561 Damage (+14)
                                                                                                                                                                  @ 70 Dex = 593 Damage (+32!!)
                                                                                                                                                                  @ 80 Dex = 615 Damage (+22!!)
                                                                                                                                                                  @ 89 Dex = 627 Damage (+12) (Going from 89 to 90 gives no more points, this is where it breaks down)
                                                                                                                                                                  @ 99 Dex = 631 Damage (+4)

                                                                                                                                                                  So for those wanting a pure dex build, don't be afraid to raise dex to beyond 60, in my testing 80 is where it appears to slow down to not worth it territory again. And with the new buff to Prisoner's Chain it's not that hard to get the points for ~70-75 dex and still keep 30+ in Vig/End.
