            Sellsword Twinblades are a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Paired scimitars used by certain sellswords. The scimitars' sharp blades make for effective slashing attacks, but fare poorly against metal armor and tough scale-covered hides.

            With a scimitar in each hand, the wielder can vary their onslaught with unique left-handed attacks when in the proper stance.

            Skill: Spin Slash
            Slice into foes with a large spinning motion, and continue spinning to transition into a strong attack



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Can perform a parry when one handed in the left hand.
            • This weapon has a high DPS when the paired attacks are used.
            • The Alpha version originally had the Legion Scimitars, which behaved like this particular weapon. It was eventually dropped in the final game, being replaced by this weapon.
            • Based off of a real life weapon called the shashka, which is a Russian hiltless backsword used during the 17~19th century.

             

            Softcaps and Scaling

            • 10/16 = 237 = Base Stats
            • 10/40 = 323 = Dex Softcap
            • This weapon receives minimal scaling from Strength



            Location/Where to Find

            • Starting equipment for the Mercenary
            • Found behind the Black Knight in the swamp area in the Road of Sacrifices. Spawning at the Halfway Fortress bonfire, go down past the Lycanthrope Hunters and stick to the right going forward until you find an entrance to a destroyed Keep. Head left and past the Black Knight. The Twinblades are down the hallway on the right. 
            • Fextra Video location (starts at 1h 5m 47s)
