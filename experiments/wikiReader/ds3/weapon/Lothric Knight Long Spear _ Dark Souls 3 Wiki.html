            Lothric Knight Long Spear is a Pike in Dark Souls 3.



            Long spear wielded by the proud Lothric Knights. Designed for long-range thrusting.  
            Lothric spear knights are known for their steadfastness, and this spear boosts the poise of its wielder.

            Skill: Charge  
            Hold spear at waist and charge at foe. Use strong attack while charging to extend the length of the charge

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Increases Poise by 15 while equipped.

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Lothric Knights wielding it.
            • Sold by Greirat after pillaging Irithyll Valley.

                                                                                                                                                                                Increased my poise from 36.27 to 42.65. (Wolf Ring +1) I can't tell if it's a multiplicative or additive bonus. That's a difference of 17.5% multiplicative, or 6.38 poise units additive. I think multiplicative makes more sense, since 17.5% is a relatively standard looking number. It's a pretty massive bonus. Even with 10 faith my dragonslayer spear does more damage because its faith scaling is pretty minuscule in this game. It also attacks faster due to having the spear moveset, while the lothric spear has the pike moveset. BUT the poise bonus is pretty serious business, assuming it's multiplicative it should make a pretty big difference for heavy armor builds, especially if you use the wolf ring. You can even get the bonus with the spear in your left hand, and a heavy weapon in your right hand. I tried this out with a black knight greataxe in my right hand, and when trading with an UGS user, I could stun him but he couldn't stun me. So you can probably do some pretty creative things with this weapon.As a right hand weapon it's actually pretty good, especially due to the sprint attacks. I make use of the sprint/guard glitch thing. I'm not even sure it's a glitch, maybe just an ordinary mechanic. Anyway you hold L1 with a shield in your left hand, or 2 handing a weapon, such that you're guarding while sprinting. Sprint a little ways, keep holding circle &amp; L1, but stop moving forward with the thumbstick. So you'll still be holding block, you'll still be holding the sprint button, but you won't be moving. Then, wait as long as you want and hit R1 or R2. If you hit R1 you'll do a sprinting attack from a stationary position. If you hit R2 you'll do a jumping attack from a stationary position. This is pretty useful for certain weapons, and this spear is one of them. There is almost zero charge up time for the sprint attack. The weakness is that when you sprint at someone, you telegraph the lance sprint attack. So this guard sprint glitch completely eliminates that one weakness. You can just stand there, with your shield up, waiting for the right time, then hit R1 and just instantly start charging at your opponent. Try it out yourself. Reminds me of lances from DS2, there were some pretty ridiculous lance strategies available to people who were willing to learn difficult mechanisms.
