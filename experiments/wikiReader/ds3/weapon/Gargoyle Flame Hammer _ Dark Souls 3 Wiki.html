            Gargoyle Flame Hammer is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Stone torch hammer wielded by gargoyles of the Profaned Capital.

            The Profaned Flame, which never goes out, imbues this weapon with a fire attack.

            Skill: Kindled Fury
            Anchor weapon in earth and use strong attack to launch successive volleys of fire from the tip of the hammer. 

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Buffed or Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Can not move or change aim during Kindled Flurry.
            • Fire damage scales with INT/FTH independently of physical attack, making this weapon potentially useful for pyromancers with some STR investment.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Rock Gargoyles using this weapon in Profaned Capital or Grand Archives.
