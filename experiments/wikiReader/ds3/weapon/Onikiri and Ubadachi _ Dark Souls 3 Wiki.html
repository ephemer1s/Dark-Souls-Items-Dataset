            Onikiri and Ubadachi is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            The hunters known as the King's Black Hands wielded paired weapons. These belonged to Kamui, who looked after the prince.

            Kamui brought Onikiri with him to the Undead Settlement, where he forged Ubadachi, and was finally prepared to join the ranks of the royal hunters.

            Skill: Onislayer
            Leap forward and slash mercilessly with both blades, cutting open foes."



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Black Hand Kamui at the top floor of the Grand Archives.

             

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Upgrades with Titanite.
            • Makes for good Quality and Hollow builds.
            • Strangely, Heavy infusion is viable on these weapons. In contrast, Sharp infusion isn't as good if your strength stat is really low.
            • Quick softcaps and base stats comparisons between str/dex heavy/sharp:
              • 13/25 sharp (base reqs) = 325 AR
              • 13/40 sharp (15 levels) = 379 AR
              • 40/25 heavy (27 levels) = 361 AR
              • 40/40 normal (42 levels) = 406 AR (sharp is 2 AR behind, heavy is same as last one)
              • 13/67 sharp (42 levels) = 415 AR
              • Going higher, even if strength is equal to dex, sharp is ahead as the dex scaling doesn't cap very hard.
              • I.e. there's little reason to level strength, and certainly no reason to go heavy.
            • Onikiri and Ubadachi literally means "ogre cutter" (鬼切) and "old lady sword" (姥断), respectively. 
            • This set of weapons is known as a daishō, which consists of the katana and wakizashi.
