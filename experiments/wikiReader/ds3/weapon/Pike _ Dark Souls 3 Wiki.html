            Pike is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Long spear with a very long hilt. Designed for long-range thrusting. Has the longest range of the non-projectile weapons, but its very length makes it unwieldy in certain situations.

            Skill: Charge
            Hold spear at waist and charge at foe. Use strong attack while charging to extend distance. 

             



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • It and all other pike-class weapons have a slow attack speed when used one handed that becomes much faster when wielded with two hands.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Branch-wielding Hollows and hollows wielding shields and spears in the Crucifixion Woods
