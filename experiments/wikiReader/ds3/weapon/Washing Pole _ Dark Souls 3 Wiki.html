            Washing Pole is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.


            A stunning, unusually long katana forged in an eastern land.

            The extreme length of this blade provides immense range, but also renders the blade extremely fragile.

            Skill: Hold
            Assume a holding stance to rapidly execute a lunging slash with normal attack, or a deflective parry with strong attack.



            Notes and Tips

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • It's the longest katana in the game, although this comes at the cost of higher stamina usage per swing.
            • 18 STR and 60 DEX nets a Sharp Washing Pole 420 AR, making the third best Katana in terms of physical damage. Second place taken by Black Blade (430 AR), and first place being the Chaos Blade (444 AR).
              • It's strongest AR comes from the Hollow infusion.
            • It is based on the ceremonial sword dachi. This may be why it is always extremely fragile in the Souls series.
            • It is named after the weapon of famous swordsman Sasaki Kojiro, who used a larger-than-normal odachi called "Laundry/Drying/Washing Pole". Kojiro was reported to be extremely fast and precise, which may explain why the weapon art (silly fast!) resembles the "Swallow Cut", which Kojiro was famous for using with his 'Laundry Pole'.
            • Does not have R1 R1 combo. 

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Sold by the Shrine Handmaid after giving her the Easterner's Ashes for 7,500 souls.
