            Chaos Blade is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A cursed sword of unknown origin bearing uncanny streaks on its blade.

            Attacks also damage its wielder.

            The sword is not unlike a thing misshappen, granted life, but never welcome in this world. In other words, chaos itself.

            Skill: Hold
            Assume a holding stance to rapidly execute a lunging slash with normal attack, or a deflecting parry with strong attack.

             

             

            Notes and Tips

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Upgrades with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Damages the wielder with each strike (≈1% of HP), but inflicts more bleeding than other Katanas and has potentially the highest damage.
            • At maximum refinement the Chaos Blade has S scaling in Dex and high base damage making it potentially the highest damaging katana in the game for Dex builds.
            • The HP loss can be countered with varying efficiency by the Sun Princess Ring, Ring of the Evil Eye, Pontiff's Left Eye and spells like Replenishment, Bountiful Light and Bountiful Sunlight.
            • Ripostes & Backstabs won't trigger the damage per strike. Useful to keep the Lloyd's Sword Ring buff.
            • At 79 LCK the Chaos Blade +5 has 51 Bleed.
            • Used by the Sword Master instead of the Uchigatana when the Sword Master is summoned at Untended Graves.



             

            Location/Where to Find

            • It is located at the top of the path to the left of the dark Firelink Shrine ahead of the Champion Gundyr/Untended Graves bonfire (it parallels the place where you fought the Sword Master  with the Uchigatana in Cemetery of Ash).

Uchigatana, Black Blade &amp; Chaos blade all have the same parry frames, frame 8.0 - 16.1 @60fps. Washing pole actually has the longest parry window, 8.0 - 16.3 @60fps. For comparison, Caetus is 8.1 - 16.3, however it has an extra 2 frame recovery time (30 vs 28.1) and the katanas get a damage absorption bonus while using the parry, making them a little safer to use
