            Lothric Knight Greatsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Ultra greatsword of the venerable knights of Lothric. Imbued with the strength of lightning, the trademark of dragon hunting.

            Very few have demonstrated the extreme strength and dexterity required for this weapon, even in the long history of the Lothric Knights.

            Skill: Stomp

            Use one's weight to lunge forward with a low stance and increased poise, and follows with strong attack for an upward slash.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused despite its natural Lightning damage
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Lightning Blade augments Light Attack spam capabilities.
            • For physical damage, Sharp works best, followed by Refined.
            • Blessed competes with Lightning Infusion at 60 Faith even in PvP.
            • Blessed, Lightning, Chaos and Dark infusions add Faith scaling to it's lightning base damage. Additionally, Chaos and Dark provide one of the highest ARs in the game at max character level.

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped from Lothric Knights carrying this weapon (Rare drop)
            • One can be found walking down the strairs directly south of the Twin Princes boss fight/bonfire. Due to the knight's proximity to a bonfire, this is a very easy place to farm for the greatsword
            • Can be purchased from Greirat of the Undead Settlement or Shrine Handmaid if you give her Greirat's Ashes. Note: Greirat's Ashes will NOT allow purchase from the Shrine Handmaiden if Greirat is killed before he leaves to scavenge the second time.
