            Broadsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A straight sword with a broad blade designed for slashing.

            A large sweeping attack makes this effective against crowds, but is prone to deflection from walls in narrow spaces.

            Skill: Stance
            While in stance, use normal attack to break a foe's guard from below, and strong attack to slash upwards with a forward lunge.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • All weapons have motion values assigned to each attack, which are modifiers to AR that are associated with the attack being used.
              • The Broadsword has a motion value of 200 on backstab, 250 on riposte, and 60+225 on Hornet Ring riposte. The motion value of a backstab with a straight sword does not change with Hornet Ring.
            • The Long Sword has a range of 32.2849xl on the 1h R1 and 31.9551xl on the 2h R1.
            • The Broadsword has lunging R2 attack that finishes with a sweep, making it good for closing distance.
            • In terms of damage, it excels over the other Straight Swords in certain areas, primarily when infused Raw, Fire, and Deep, as well as Heavy on their respective builds.
              • This makes it very popular on low-level and sl1 playthroughs.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted from corpse in High Wall of Lothric. From the Tower on the Wall Bonfire, progress normally until you reach the platform with a Hollow Soldier with a crossbow. Instead of going down the ladder, you turn towards the other room, with a patrolling Lothric Knight. Past them and to the left is another Hollow Soldier lying in wait, and in the room they were hiding in is the Broadsword, behind some destroyable crates.
