            Darkdrift is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A cursed sword with an unseen blade, the choice weapon of Yuria of Londor.

            Yuria, a mentor of the Sable Church and accomplished swordswoman, is said to have claimed a hundred lives with this weapon. 

            Skill: Darkdrift
            Aim carefully, and pierce with a large forward lunge. The unseen blade penetrates straight through shields, for nothing can defend from Darkdrift.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused but can be Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • The blade of this weapon appears translucent in-game and when in another world as a phantom or dark spirit, it's possible to see through your character by placing the blade between the camera and the player character.
            • Lowest stamina consumption of all Katanas.
            • The weapon fits well into a Quality build. Gets good DEX scaling up to 40 DEX, good STR scaling up to 36 STR. Softcaps at 40/40 STR/DEX with 383 AR.
              • Apparently, 24 Strength seems to be the highest sweetspot for DEX builds.
            • Has  innate shield pierce: 30% on heavy attacks, 100% on the Weapon Skill, and 10% on all other attacks.

            Softcaps & Scaling:

            • 10/28 = 309 AR = Base Stats
            • 40/28 = 351 AR = Str Softcap
            • 10/40 = 342 AR = Dex Softcap
            • 40/40 = 383 AR = Quality Build



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Yuria of Londor at Firelink Shrine.
            • If you summon Yuria of Londor for the last boss fight and you succeed with Yuria alive, Darkdrift and the full black set can be found where Yuria usually stands in Firelink.
