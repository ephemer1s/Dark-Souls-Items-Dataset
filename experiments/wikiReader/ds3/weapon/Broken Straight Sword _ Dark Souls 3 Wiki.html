            Broken Straight Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A straight sword with a broken blade.

            A weapon with no exceptional qualities. Only a mad Hollow would choose to fight with this.

            Skill: Stance
            While in stance, use normal attack to break a foe's guard from below, and strong attack to slash upwards with a forward lunge. Only, neither move will appear very impressive with a broken sword.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite
            • All weapons have motion values assigned to each attack, which are modifiers to AR that are associated with the attack being used.
              • The Broken Straight Sword has a motion value of 200 on backstab, 250 on riposte, and 60+225 on Hornet Ring riposte. The motion value of a backstab with a straight sword does not change with Hornet Ring.
            • The Broken Straight Sword is the shortest straight sword, with a range of 29.209xl 1h and a range of 28.5449xl 2h.
            • The Broken Straight Sword has lunging R2 attack that finishes with a sweep.
            • This is essentially a joke weapon, but can get decent enough damage to be viable. Its short reach is the real drawback of the weapon, as it can miss some enemies at close range.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Firelink Shrine: Looted from gravestone just through the doorway after the first boss, Iudex Gundyr. (Location Video)
            • Dropped by Hollow in High Wall of Lothric.
