            Yorshka's Chime is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A sacred chime belonging to Yorshka. Her brother, the former knight captain, presented her with this medium together with another gift: her name.
            The ringing of the chime must have done much to soothe her loneliness.

            Skill: Gentle Prayer
            Recovers HP for a period of time, albeit extremely slowly. Works while equipped in either hand.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • After 49 Faith, this Chime has the highest Spell Buff.
            • At 55 Faith, this Chime has a Spell Buff of 228, and 247 SB at 60 Faith.
            • The chime has to remain active in your hand for the weapon art to continue regenerating health.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Drops from Company Captain Yorshka if killed.

                                                                                                                                                                                Best chime for buffing weapons with miracles. Faith does increase your spell buff based on the chimes faith scaling, and in turn increases damage from buffing. The math works like this for Lightning and Darkmoon blade miracles:

                                                                                                                                                                                .95*(spell buff)=attack rating increase.

                                                                                                                                                                                That means 95% of your spell buff will become either lightning or magical damage. At a 60 faith investment that gives almost 230 damage per hit before damage reductions and absorptions. Or in reality, about 95-125 to enemy players after if they fill all their armor slots. Needless to say, best used on fast weapons.
