            Great Machete is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            This giant machete has a slash attack, and is a worker's tool in the Undead Settlement.

            This dismantling tool was not originally intended for use in battle.

            Skill: Sharpen
            Scrape the blade to sharpen and enable a cleaner cut.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Neither this nor Yhorm's Great Machete  have a guaranteed combo while two handing. This has to be a bug or an odd way to balance the weapon since every other weapon has a guaranteed combo.

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Drops from large undead wielding this weapon (with planks and cages on their backs) in Undead Settlement and Road of Sacrifices.
