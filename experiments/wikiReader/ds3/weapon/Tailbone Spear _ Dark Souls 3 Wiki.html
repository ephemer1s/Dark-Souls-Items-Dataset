            Tailbone Spear is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Twisted spear favored by the wretched, failed prisoners of Irithyll Dungeon.

            Crafted from a feeble tailbone, and seen as a sign of the dragons. Their screams are oft heard inside the dungeon, as they naively mutilate their unworthy flesh.

            Skill: Unleash Dragon
            Thrust the spear to unleash the strength of dragons, similar to an ancient dragon weapon, only with its power tragically faded.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite Scales.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Drops very rarely from the Wretches in Irithyll Dungeon that wield this weapon.
