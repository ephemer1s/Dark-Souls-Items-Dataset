            Great Scythe is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Attacks with this large scythe, normally used for crop harvesting, are effective at breaking past shields.

            The magnificent sharp curved blade instills fear in opponents. Perhaps it is their survival instinct at work.

            Skill: Neck Swipe
            This attack aims for the scruff of a foe's neck, and when successful, functions as a head shot, inflicting heavy damage.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Has innate shield pierce: 40% on all attacks and 80% on the weapon art.
            • Has a "Sweet Spot" meaning if the play hit with the pole part of the weapon it does 5% less damage, conversely if the player hits with the blade they deal 3% more damage. 

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted from Undead Settlement in a destroyed building near the rain of giant arrows and the White Birch Tree.
