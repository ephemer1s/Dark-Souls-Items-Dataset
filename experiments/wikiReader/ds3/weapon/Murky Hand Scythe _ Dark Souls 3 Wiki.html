            Murky Hand Scythe is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3. It was added in The Ringed City DLC.



            A short-shafted hand scythe wielded by the murkmen who rise from the depths.

            Enveloped by a black dampness, and imbued with the strength of dark

            Skill: Quickstep
            Instantly step behind or around the side of foes. Especially effective when locked on to target.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Scales better with Chaos Gem, However is better with Dark Gem due to innate dark.
            • All weapons have motion values assigned to each attack, which are modifiers to AR that are associated with the attack being used.
              • The Harpe has a motion value of 300 on backstab, 360 on riposte, and 60+350 on Hornet Ring riposte. The motion value of a backstab  with a dagger does not change with Hornet Ring.
              • This means that aside from a difference in AR, no infusion is strictly better for ripostes, as all infusions will get the same motion value, with the only difference being AR. Use whatever infusion will net the most damage for the best results.
              • Note that dark damage does not benefit from Hornet Ring in PvP.
            • Murky Hand Scythe has a range of 24.3255xl on the 1h R1 and 26.0078xl on the 2h R1
            • The Weapon Skill Quickstep can be used to lessen the recovery frames of any attack.
            • Despite having the normal dagger moveset, the Harpe and Murky Hand Scythe have the hit stun properties of a katana on the 2h lunging R1, or first 2h R1 out of neutral.
            • The Murky Hand Scythe loses a lot of physical damage when Dark infused, and loses a lot of dark damage when Chaos infused, due to a previous nerf. 
            • The Murky Hand Scythe gains more AR on all standard builds (66 Strength build, 75-80 Dex build, 60 Int build, 60 Faith build, 30/30 Int/Faith build) when compared to Harpe, and can generally be considered a straight upgrade, aside from the extra requirements in Int and Faith, range, and a few niche builds or infusions (such as Blessed on a 60 Faith build).
              • The ability to buff this weapon makes it exceptional on a Strength-Faith hybrid build, and gives it an edge with Human Pine Resin.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Found on a corpse in The Dreg Heap, after dropping through the glass ceiling. It's up some stairs over a collapsed bookcase laying on its side.
