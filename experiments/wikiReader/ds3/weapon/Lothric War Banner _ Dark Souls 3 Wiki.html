            Lothric War Banner is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3. It is part of The Ringed City.



            The flagpole that once carried the Lothric crest, and guided the knights long ago.

            The tip of the pole is fitted with a sharply pointed decoration, letting it serve as a spear.

            Skill: Lothric War Banner
            Wave the flagpole high in the air, like the days of yore, summoning an ethereal war banner that invigorates the attack of those who gather under it.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • The buff increases all damage by 15%
            • The Weapon Art buff last approximately 2 minutes, and behaves like a lingering, mobile AOE, applying and removing itself as allies enter and exit its radius. 
            • WA buff only lingers while the weapon is actively held in either/both hand(s), and is removed upon applying any other weapon buff. 
            • Stacks with other buffs like Oath of Sunlight. 
            • Warning; While under the effect of WA most enemies will be alerted to you. Even if approaching them from behind. Tested with Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring.
            • WA buff duration is not increased with Lingering Dragoncrest Rings.

            Location/Where to Find

            • A hidden area just shortly after you meet Lapp for the first time (before Earthen Peak Ruins bonfire). You will see a bridge which you can drop down from onto a Harald enemy, however do not drop down. To the right of the small bridge there is a spot you can drop from safely into the ash below. There you will find a group of enemies. Past them is another spot where you can drop down safely. You'll find two knights here, and past them you'll find the corpse and a safe drop to the next bonfire.
