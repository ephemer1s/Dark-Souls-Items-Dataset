            Soldering Iron is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.


            Branding iron used by the jailers prowling Irithyll Dungeon.

            Press its searing tip against foes to inflict fire damage, and temporarily block Estus healing.

            Skill: Charge
            Hold iron at waist and charge at foe. Use strong attack while charging to extend the length of the charge.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • On hit, decreases human opponents' Estus healing back to its base level.
            • The description states that the weapon blocks Estus healing, however, this is false. It will only decrease the Estus' healing level by 20%
            • The effect applied by the weapon lasts 15 seconds. This does not seem to be affected by the Lingering Dragoncrest Ring.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Drops from Jailors in Irithyll Dungeon.
