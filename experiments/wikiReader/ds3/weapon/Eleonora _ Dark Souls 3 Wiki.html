            Eleonora is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A strange weapon found among malformed inhabitants of the Profaned Capital.

            The Profaned Flame was triggered by the curse of these women, relatives of a certain oracle, but despite their culpability, they went on living, without any cares.

            Skill: Feast Bell
            Hold up axe and wave to emit a solemn chime to temporarily make weapons lacerating, and to restore HP for each hit.



            Notes and Tips

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite Scale.
            • At 40/40, it has 421 AR.
            • Can traded with Pickle Pee, Pump-a-Rum for a Hollow Gem.
            • After use of weapon art, heal 15 on hit.
            • Buff last about 25 seconds



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by the first Monstrosity of Sin  to aggro you in the bottom floor of the church by the swamp in the Profaned Capital.

             

            Trivia

            • This weapon shares the name of a short story written by Edgar Allen Poe.
