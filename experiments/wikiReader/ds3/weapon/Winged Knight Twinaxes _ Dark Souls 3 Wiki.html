            Winged Knight Twinaxes is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Paired beheading axes wielded by the Winged Knights, who swore themselves to the Angels.

            These axes, more befitting of an executioner than a knight, are indented to fit the human body.

            Skill: Chain Spin
            Sweep foes in a large spinning motion and use strong attack to continue the motion until stamina is exhausted.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Winged Knights wielding this weapon (uncommon).
