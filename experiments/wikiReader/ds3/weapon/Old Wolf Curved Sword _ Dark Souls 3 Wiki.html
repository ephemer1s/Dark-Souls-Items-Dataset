            Old Wolf Curved Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.


             

            Curved sword bearing the soul of the old wolf that stays with the Watchdogs of Farron.

            This sword, like a wolf on the prowl, boosts attack and restores HP with each consecutive hit.

            Skill: Wolf Leap
            Slice into foes with a large spinning motion, then leap out of harm's way and follow through with a strong attack.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Boosts attacks, as long as attacking persists. Stacks with Pontiff's Right Eye Ring & Carthus Beacon
              • PVE: Initial Buff = 5%, Max Buff = 10%
              • PVP: Initial Buff = 3%, Max Buff = 6%
            • Recovers Hp, with consecutive hits. Stacks with Pontiff's Left Eye Ring 
              • 120 HP healed.
            • Both effects are also activated when landing hits with other weapons while the Old Wolf Curved Sword is equipped.
            • You do not need to meet minimum requirements for the effects to trigger.
            • Does not stack with a second Old Wolf Curved Sword.

             

            Softcaps & Scaling:

            • 19/25 = 410 AR = Base Stats
            • 40/25 = 447 AR = Str Softcap
            • 19/40 = 496 AR = Dex Softcap
            • 19/60 = 523 AR = Dex Hardcap
            • 40/40 = 533 AR = Quality Build
            • 40/60 = 560 AR

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Received by reaching Rank 1 of Watchdogs of Farron. (You must hand in 10 Wolf's Blood Swordgrass)

             

            Lore

            • More lore about Artorias and The Abyss Watchers.
