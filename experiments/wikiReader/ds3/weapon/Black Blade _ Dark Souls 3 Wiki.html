            Black Blade is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A short katana wielded by the swordsman and distinguished guest of High Lord Wolnir. This shiny black blade is thick, but shorter than the typical katana.

            The swordsman was a master of a rare technique, traces of which can be observed in this weapon's strong attack.

            Skill: Hold
            Assume a holding stance to rapidly execute a lunging slash with a normal attack, or a deflecting parry with a strong attack.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • The shortest katana in the game, but has the highest base damage out of all katanas.
              The Darkdrift follows close behind.
            • Has unique heavy attack moveset.
              • One-handed heavy attacks are a horizontal slash followed by a regular thrust.
              • 2H it uses two downwards vertical slashes, the first one holding the tip of the blade.
            • The Black Blade is the only Katana that can "pancake" (knockdown) an opponent.
              Exact pancake circumstances require testing:
              • Hold --> R1 
              • Two handed R2
              • ??? 
            • Despite being the shortest katana, it's moveset compensates the effective range.
              • Both 1H heavy attacks feature a significant forward step not found in other katanas.
              • Rolling 1H heavy attacks can deal significant counter damage with proper spacing.
              • The unique 2H heavy attack lacks forward-step, but is convenient for roll-catching.
            • It's R1 combo into roll-catching WA heavy attack can deal over 800 damage.
            • At 40/40 Dex/Luck, a hollow infused Black Blade has an AR of 423/440 1H/2H.
            • This weapon resembles the Berserker Blade from Dark Souls 2.
            • The weapon is possibly named after a cancelled PS3 title with the same name, also developed by FromSoftware.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted from a Mimic chest near the Flame Demon, on the way to Smouldering Lake.

                                                                                                                                                                                                  So, what is the Black Blade?It is a Katana that loves to headshot. The most remarkable thing about the weapon is its 2-handed strong attack which executes a stylish, and perfect, downward slash. You can land a strike to the head with that slash while being at the same hight (no steppes-tactics, etc. needed) as your opponent. That is Black Blade's main advantage. Regarding AR values, an infused BB deals more burst damage than Uchigatana, but is less deadly in comparison to Washing Pole. That being said, it is a "mid-tier" Katana. Headshot multiplier deals considerable amount of damage, but is pretty circumstantial; however, not as circumstantial as the weapon Skill of Reapers...Attack speed is the same as Uchi's or Washpole's, with only one exception: one-handed strong attacks are a little (very little) swifter and have a different pattern. BB at first lends a horizontal slash and then thrusts, while Uchi (and WP) does that in an opposite order. The main disadvantage of the Black Blade is its range. In comparison to Washpole's, it is close to nonexistent. Running poke attack is very dangerous to its wielder as you will most likely be caught in a stun-lock attack sequence, because opponent's reach will be superior to yours.I like the Black Blade mostly for its sturdy look, and a pair of stylish, useful attacks, but otherwise it is sitting somewhere in the middle of Katana class. It can be really good, and occasionally menacing, but requires a lot of practice and swift positioning to perform to its fullest potential.
