            Partizan is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Spear with blade attached to broad point. Long reach, and can be used with shield up. Attacks faster with shield down.

            This weapon can utilize its blade for slicing attacks, and is generally adaptable to many situations, but in cramped quarters, its slices ricochet off walls.

            Skill: Spin Sweep
            Sweep foes in a large spinning motion, and utilize momentum to transition into an overhanded strong attack smash.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Has horizontal slashes for its Heavy attacks. Among spear class weapons, this is shared only with Yorshka's Spear and the Ringed Knight Spear.
            • The weapon art is a true combo from a 2 handed heavy attack.
            • R2 R2 is a true combo. 



            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted from a hanging corpse near where Cornyx is being held, above the third bonfire at the Undead Settlement. (Watch the Video)

                      This is my all time favorite weapon. The only downside in partizan is it's average reach. You really have to learn your spacing with this thing as it is very easy to be too close or too far away from your opponent. Timing is also important especially when utilizing leo ring because typical greatswords have similar reach than partizan, and you don't want to engage trading with this weapon. You also have to be wary of parries and play smart.

                      R2 horizontal sweeps make Partizan very fun to wield. Crowd control as well as rollcatching is made easy. Standard damage output is nice against poke-resistant foes. R2 to R2 also comboes in most cases.

                      The true beauty lies in the versatility. Weapon art is unparryable and even has hyperarmor to some extend. Additionally after the initial swing, you can very quickly use R1 instead of the extended R2. This makes your moves more unpredictable, which is nice because ill timed Spin Sweep leaves you wide open due to the final strike being vertical slam.

                      Jump attack has good reach and can be followed up with rolling attack, which is quite fast and reachy. Basic spear feature.

                      I like to use a Bandit Knife in my offhand to counter opponents from getting too close. No glorious damage but dagger swings interrupt other fast weapons nicely.                                                                                                                                                       Average damage, though slightly higher base damage than the basic Spear, Winged Spear, and Drang Twinspears. Same base damage as the Tailbone Spear, but the Partizan is infusable, is accessible earlier, and is easier to upgrade, so it's more flexible with your build.Average range.A unique mix of sweeps and thrusts allows you to easily handle situations that regular spears struggle with, such as managing several spread out enemies. The timing on the sweep attacks is also different enough from the pokes to throw off opponents in PvP.The Weapon Art is the elephant in the room; it's flashy and will impress onlookers, but the damage is pathetic. Over 3 hits it deals the same damage as 1 R1, is slower, uses more stamina, and FP. If you want to hit multiple opponents without needing to line them up, just use the R2s more damage, and faster. The weapon art is completely ignorable.
