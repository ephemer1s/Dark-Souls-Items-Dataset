            Morne's Great Hammer is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.  



            Greathammer bestowed upon Carim Knights who demonstrate outstanding strength and unwavering faith. Decorated by a ward of Carim Temple, and imbued with the twisted rage of Apostle Morne.

            Skill: Morne's Rage
            Stick weapon into earth and emit a powerful shockwave. Also, similar to Perseverance, temporarily boosts poise and reduces damage received.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Upgraded with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Being a weapon with some of the highest stat requirements, this is one of the far less viable weapons on a SL 120 character. 
            • Visually similar to Bramd from Demon's Souls, and has the same stat requirements as the Grant from Dark Souls 1.
            • At +5 with 50 STR/40 FTH, this weapon has 631 AR.
            • Added Faith scaling and had Strength scaling altered in Regulation 1.09 patch, making it a good STR/FTH weapon. Before then, it only had a B scaling in Strength at +5.
            • This Great Hammer, along with Smough's Great Hammer  and Ledo's Great Hammer, have a higher poise health value than the other Great Hammers, with 32.9 poise health instead of the usual 30.8. This means you can have much less poise and still be able to tank through the other Ultras.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Eygon of Carim if killed.

            • Looted from Eygon of Carim's corpse late-game in the old cell of Irina of Carim.

                                                                                                                              This is a pretty good weapon, though the FP cost requires a faith/str caster setup (I'm doing alright around 35 fp, mileage may vary to taste). This weapon art makes the Farron Ring worth it (32 vs 50). Run this with a talisman in the offhand for buffs and healing (since you won't be able to hold much else unless you go heavy in vitality or use Havel's ring). Now for the fun stats:

                                                                                                                              Matches Smough's Greathammer (668 AR) at 66 str (2h) and 60 faith. At 66/99, it reaches 684 AR.

                                                                                                                              Weapon art fires off perseverance instantly after pulling the trigger as the animation begins, guaranteeing that if you can start the art, it will finish. Due to animation length, you won't get much benefit from perseverance (inability to get staggered) once you can move again, so be wary of people rushing at the end of the animation. You should get perseverance's falloff absorption boost for ~.5 seconds once you can move again.

                                                                                                                              Weapon art damage scales with faith AND weapon upgrade. Upgrade level affects WA damage the most, faith damage scales (marginally) better at higher upgrade levels. Also, seems? to softcap around 60 faith:

                                                                                                                              Mourn +4 @ 30 faith: WA = 894 dmg
                                                                                                                              Mourn +4 @ 60 faith: WA = 948 dmg

                                                                                                                              Mourn +5 @ 30 faith: WA = 1038 dmg
                                                                                                                              Mourn +5 @ 60 faith: WA = 1098 dmg

                                                                                                                              I tried a +5 @ 99 faith and it was around 1120. All tests were run on NG @ Anor Londo. Baited knights to charge and fired off WA as I got hit (point blank hits). When testing the +4 one didn't lightning charge and got a shield up. They were still flattened, but only took about 630 damage. Don't know how (or if it's possible) to edit these, so posting here. If someone knows how to or can incorporate some of this into the main page, please do so! Thanks, -Gamma Lead
