            Immolation Tinder is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Profaned Flame wielded by the Irithyllian witches, frigid spirits roaming the Boreal Valley.

            This torch, both a weapon and a staff, is enshrouded in an everlasting flame.

            Skill: Punitive Flame
            Punish foes with a flame that blankets the ground.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Can be used to cast Sorceries.
              • Cannot cast Pyromancies however, contrary to its appearance and usage by the enemies that wield it.
            • Longer range while one-handed.
            • Higher damage and hyper armor while two-handed.
            • At 60 INT it has a spellbuff of 220.
            • Farron Flashsword does not present a big upgrade to range, but it is an efficient quick attack.
            • Soul Greatsword does not have any hyper armor, but it does cover a huge area around the player.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Drops from Fire Witches in Irithyll of the Boreal Valley.

                                                                                                                                                      Arguably the most versatile catalyst in the game, and potentially as deadly as the mother of all Halberds: GH. While you don't have GH's INSANE homing-sweep charge, you do gain an additional ranged attack with Punitive Flame, and though the damage is somewhat low (as is the WA cast speed), Punitive Flame does scale with INT, and has proven to be on par with unbuffed Chaos Fire Orbs immediate damage (Not the lingering lava damage, so Pyros can breathe easy) In the end, this affords any sorcerer build with an INT scaling fire based spell without consuming an attunement slot. Combine the WA with the Immolation Tinder's ability to interrupt its own combos with Farron Spamsword, and you've got yourself a killer at any range.

                                                                                                                                                      The melee damage is obscene for a caster weapon, as the Immolation Tinder easily outclasses the MGS, even when MGS is being buffed by Scholar's Candlestick and Crown of Dusk. Also, the Immolation Tinder is 0.5 pounds lighter than the MGS, and fulfills the role of a catalyst. So technically, the Immolation Tinder is 2.5 pounds lighter than the equivalent MGS and Court Sorcerer's Staff.

                                                                                                                                                      Spellbuff isn't top class, but it most certainly maintains a position ahead of middle class.

                                                                                                                                                      Makes for a better torch than the Torch, and having a weaponized light source in Dark Souls is always a perk.

                                                                                                                                                      All in all, the Immolation Tinder is one of my personal favorites, well worth a Titanite Slab. There is no shortage of tricks that this Halberd can pull off. Easily the most unpredictable weapon in PVP, even mores o than TPGS, as you can go from melee to casting at the flip of a switch. There's nothing quite like beating your opponent into retreat, only to nuke them with Crystal Soul Spear the instant they go for the Estus.

                                                                                                                                                      One of the peculiar things I've noted in regards to this weapon pertains to Farron Spamsword. Unlike traditional catalysts, the Immolation Tinder's Farron Spamswords can be cancelled prematurely when hitting walls. Also: I've noticed an "orange" aura afflicting foes hit by Farron Spamswords cast by the Immolation Tinder. Given its unusual mechanics, I'm left to wonder if the Immolation Tinder's Farron Flashswords can proc a burning effect on foes. Given the infrequency of this "orange" aura, testing for Flashsword synergizing with the Immolation Tinder's burn proc has proven inconclusive.
