            Dagger is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Standard small dagger.

            Small daggers lack power or reach, but can deal quick consecutive hits due to their light weight. Highly effective when used for critical hits, such as after parrying or attacking from behind.

            Skill: Quickstep
            Instantly step behind or around the side of foes. Especially effective when locked on to target.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • The Dagger has the highest critical stat for all weapons, making it a popular reposte tool.
              • Contrary to popular belief, "Chaos" isn't the objectively best infusion, and this weapon should instead be infused for your primary damage stat (sharp for dex, chaos/dark for int/faith, etc.) However, it's usually better to invest in split damage than pure damage, making a simple infusion a great idea for a physical class that uses a lot of weapon arts, as it gives a few seconds of free FP regeneration during a reposte.
            • All weapons have motion values assigned to each attack, which are modifiers to AR that are associated with the attack being used.
              • The Dagger has a motion value of 390 on backstab, 468 on riposte, and 78+455 on Hornet Ring riposte. The motion value of a backstab  with a dagger does not change with Hornet Ring.
              • This means that aside from a difference in AR, no infusion is strictly better for ripostes, as all infusions will get the same motion value, with the only difference being AR. Use whatever infusion will net the most damage for the best results.
              • Note that Dark damage does not benefit from Hornet Ring in PvP.
              • Motion values vary on a per weapon basis, however the Dagger does have the highest motion value of all weapons for backstabs and ripostes.
            •  Contrary to popular belief the dagger is not the shortest dagger, and it has a range of 22.4025xl on the 1h R1 and 25.6669xl on the 2h R1
            • The Weapon Skill Quickstep can be used to lessen the recovery frames of any attack.
            • When in the offhand, the moveset of the basic attack changes to a stab, however since it's slower than the standard moveset with short range it's worse than the standard moveset.
