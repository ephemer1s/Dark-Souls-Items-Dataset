            Rose of Ariandel is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A flail used by the bulbous Father of the Painted World to shred his own skin, producing blood to appease the flame. Both a weapon and a miracle catalyst.

            Ariandel, being the restorer of the Painted World, knew that it was painted with blood, and only blood could protect the secret.

            Skill: Awakening

            Violently flail oneself to trigger an awakening that temporarily boosts the strength of miracles, just as the good Father used the rose himself.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Not Buffable nor Infusable.
            • Upgrades with Titanite Scales.
            • Casts Miracles on strong attack.
            • Faith scaling softcaps at 40 for casting and physical damage.
            • At 40 Faith, the total Spellbuff is 189.
            • Despite being classified as a whip weapon, it has the moveset of a small club; however, it cannot backstab or riposte just like the other whips. It has no strong attacks, but can still do a jump attack.
            • As of update 1.33, all of this weapon's attacks are now unparriable just like other whip weapons. However, it no longer has poise on its two-handed attacks.
            • Its design is based off of a Cat o' nine tails, a multi-tailed whip used for corporal punishment.
            • Its weapon art boosts Miracle damage by ~25% and lasts for two minutes.
            • Has a true combo into force, but cannot true combo into ANY other miracle.



             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Obtained by Soul Transposition of the Soul of Sister Friede.

                                                                                                                                Out of curiosity, I tried out the weapon art buff on the Pus O'Man enemies in the Consumed King's Garden since they have pretty high health pools. Testing without any miracle-boosting rings and with the Canvas Talisman +10, the damage of Sunlight Spear increases by 124.7%, with Morne's only 123.9%, with Ring of the Sun's First born only 123.5%, and with both 121.4%. The buff lasts for 2 minutes and even after you switch for another weapon, but the Lingering Dragoncrest Ring has no effect on its duration. &gt; After playing with it as an secondary, I found that the weapon art is REALLY worth having if you use lightning miracles for PvE and invasions (since the duration is pretty damn long). Make no mistake, this is the miracle-caster's Scholar's Candlestick. &gt; Other fun facts: the ends of the whip are reminiscent of Father Ariandel hands during the cutscene of the boss, earning it the nickname "Daddy's Backscratcher," casting Lightning Arrow with it and holding it makes the bow look like it has dreadlocks, and given the game's physics, it is probably the most fun weapons to block spam when two-handing.
