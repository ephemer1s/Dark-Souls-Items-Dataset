            Canvas Talisman is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Medium for casting miracles of the Gods. Canvas talismans are austere items carried by heralds.

            Equip a talisman or a sacred chime to cast miracles. Miracles must be attuned at a bonfire before use.

            Skill: Unfaltering Prayer
            Temporarily increase poise while casting miracles, preventing enemy attacks from interrupting prayer. Works while equipped in either hand.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Has the highest spellbuff of all Talismans when Faith is between 33 and 44, making it good for softcap builds. It also has the highest spellbuff of both Talismans and Chimes at 15 and 18 Faith respectively.
            • At 60 FTH it has a spellbuff of 220.
            • Great talisman for PvP when casting miracles such as Lighting Stake and Lightning Storm with Unfaltering Prayer.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Purchased from Shrine Handmaid after giving her Paladin's Ashes
