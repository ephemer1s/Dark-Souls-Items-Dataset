            Warden Twinblades is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Paired weapon wielded by the grave wardens of the Cathedral of the Deep.

            The grave wardens of the cathedral, who put down reincarnating corpses, wield weapons that cause profuse bleeding, for the loss of blood and bodily fluids is said to slow reanimation.

            Skill: Spin Slash
            Slice into foes with a large spinning motion, and continue spinning to transition into strong attack.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused
            • Upgrades with Titanite
            • Efficient with blood infusion.
            • When held in the offhand, pressing L2 will cause the Warden Twinblades to perform a parry.

             

            Softcaps and Scaling

            • 10/18 = 224 = Base Stats
            • 10/40 = 283 = Dex Softcap
            • 40/18 = 275 = Strength Softcap
            • 40/40 = 334 = Combined Softcap
            • These weapons receive poor Strength scaling

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Cathedral Grave Wardens, outside Cathedral of the Deep and Untended Graves.
