            Demon's Scar is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3. It is part of The Ringed City.



            This chaotic thing, the last flame kindled by a demon prince, is shaped like the claw marks of a demon.

            It is both a fiery bladed weapon, and pyromancy flame.

            Skill: Spin Slash
            Spin to stoke a fierce chaos flame, and use momentum to transition into a spinning strong attack, creating a short-lived lava pool.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite Scale.
            • The weapon doubles as a Pyromancy Flame
              • Heavy attack = Cast Pyromancy
              • Light attack = Light Attack
              • Does not have any regular heavy attacks except jumping attack
              • Same moveset in off-hand
            • Normal Attacks
              • All Fire damage, no physical damage
              • Boosted by Fire Clutch Ring
              • Unaffected by Flynn's Ring.
              • Unaffected by Witch's Ring and Great Swamp rings (though casting is boosted)
            • It is the lightest Curved Sword in the game.
            • Has the highest Critical damage out of all the Curved Swords.
              • Critical modifier may effect Sacred Flame
              • Only limited testing, but the critical also appears to affect critical attack spells, such as Sacred Flame. (Minor testing was accomplished on the drakeblood knights, with both a +10 Demon's Scar and a +10 Pyromancy Flame and 40/40 Int/Fai.)
            • High Fire Block %, lightweight option for Smouldering Lake Lava.
            • R1 true combo's into Black Flame and Great Combustion with a high enough casting speed.

             

             

             Spellbuff:

            • Scales with Intelligence and Faith, does not scale with Dexterity
            • 17/17 = 127
            • 20/20 = 136
            • 30/30 = 166
            • 40/40 = 190
            • 99/99 = 220

             

             

             Scaling & Softcaps:

            • 1 and 2-handed stats:
              • 0/16/17/17 = 235 AR = Base Stats & Dex Softcap
              • 0/40/17/17 = 249 AR
              • 0/16/39/17 = 290 AR = Int Softap
              • 0/16/17/39 = 290 AR = Fth Softcap
              • 0/16/39/39 = 345 AR = Combined Softcap
              • 0/40/40/40 = 361 AR 
            • After softcaps, it gets less than 2 AR per level
            • Int/Fth Weapon
              • It scales well with Intelligence and Faith
              • Dexterity softcaps at base stats

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Obtained via Soul Transposition with the Soul of the Demon Prince
