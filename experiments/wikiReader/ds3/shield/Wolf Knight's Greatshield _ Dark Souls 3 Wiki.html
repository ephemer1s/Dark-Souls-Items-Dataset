            Wolf Knight's Greatshield is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            Greatshield of a knight tainted by the dark of the Abyss, and master of the wolf's blood of Farron.

            Boasts consistent defense and divine protection against various status effects.

            Skill: Weapon Skill
            Equipping this shield in the left hand allows one to perform the Skill of the right hand weapon.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed but not Infused.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Provides an average block of 70.6%.
            • Gives 50 to all status effect resistances while equiped.

            Location/Where to Find

            • Rank 2 reward from Watchdogs of Farron Covenant. Offer 30 Wolf's Blood Swordgrass to Old Wolf of Farron.

            Lore

            • Artorias is tied to The Abyss Watchers of DS3.

                                                                                                                  Long story: This Shield was not wielded by any Abysswatcher's or any Watchdog of Farron. In Fact this shield belonged to a legendary warrior of the first Kingdom (Lordran) of the Dark Souls Series, Artorias The Abysswalker. Artorias the Abysswalker was one of the Four Knights of Gwyn (King of Lordran), as his unbreakable resolve furthered his hatred of the Dark, Specifically Darkwraiths of Kaathe. He earned the Title 'Abysswalker' due to the ability of his ring allowing him to enter and hunt down Darkwraiths. However when he heard of Oolacile being attacked by the Abyss created by the Father of the Abyss, Manus. In an effort to save the Princess of Oolacile he and his partner Sif the Greywolf made a stand against Manus, which they unfortunately lost. In a act of selflessness Artorias Jumps in front of Sif with his great shield Protecting his companion from the Abyss at the Cost of his own Sanity.
                                                                                                                  His Legacy had continued as by the rise of Lothric, The Undead Legion of Farron (Abysswathers), partook of wolf blood and it was rumored their souls were bound to the wolf blood master. Even further the fighting style and weapons of the Abysswatchers were created after his own and fought in erratic and wild styles similar to Artorias the Abysswalker's own.

                                                                                                                  Short: Artorias the Abysswalker (Wolf Knight in this case, due to Dark Souls 3), wielded the shield not a Abysswatcher of Farron. The Watchdogs of Farron follow his original legacy, by Pursuing the Abyss. It was rumored that unlike normal Knights, Artorias wielded his blade in his shield arm (Left Arm).
