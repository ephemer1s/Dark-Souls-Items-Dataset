            Greatshield of Glory is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            "A greatshield adorned with flowing patterns, a celebration of an ancient glory. Boasts the highest stability of such shields, but with reduced stamina regeneration speed. Perhaps it is glory that begets such indolence."

            Skill: Shield Bash
            Without lowering your guard, strike the enemy with the shield to knock them back or stagger them. Works while equipped in either hand.





            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused but can be Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Decreases stamina regeneration rate by -20 while equipped.  
            • When fully upgraded it has 85 stability, the highest in the whole game.  
            • Casting Magic Shield on it when fully upgraded will bring it's stability to 100, 
              resulting in no stamina loss when blocking but elemental damage will chip through.
            • Compared to Havel's Greatshield +5 (80 stability) and this shield +5 (85 stability) the stamina
              consumption is 25% less when blocking attacks with this shield.     
            • This shield was used by the Drakekeepers of DS2.                      



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by a Mimic in the Profaned Capital (near boss fog).
