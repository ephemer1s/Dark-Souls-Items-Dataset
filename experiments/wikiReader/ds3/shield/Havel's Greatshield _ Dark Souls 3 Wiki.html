            Havel's Greatshield is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            A tremendously solid and heavy greatshield, cut straight from a great slab of stone.

            Said to be a relic of the legendary Havel the Rock, along with the Dragon Tooth. The shield is imbued with a special power reminiscent of Havel himself.

            Skill: Stone Flesh

              Raise the shield in silent prayer, turning the user's body into a solid mass of stone.





            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused but can be Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Provides an average block of 86.2%, the highest of all greatshields, but it is also the heaviest of all greatshields. Weighing 8 units more than the majority, it's a bit of an "Ultra Greatshield".
            • Has the highest elemental resistances out of all the Greatshields.

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by the Havel Knight wielding it in Archdragon Peak.
