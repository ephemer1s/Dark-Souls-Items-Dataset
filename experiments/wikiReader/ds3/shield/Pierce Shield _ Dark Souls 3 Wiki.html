            Pierce Shield is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            An unusual style of round shield peculiar to the knights of Catarina.

            The center of the shield features a large, sharpened spike, which is used to skewer opponents.

            Skill: Shield Bash
            Without lowering your guard, strike the enemy with the shield to knock them back or stagger them. Works while equipped in either hand.





            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Provides an decent average damage block of 56.8. The best of the basher shields, with great stability for its weight. If you don't employ a shield bash in your fighting style, this shield is not recommended.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Purchased from Unbreakable Patches after defeating Deacons of the Deep.
            • Drops from Siegward of Catarina when you meet him first in the Giant's tower in Undead Settlement

            Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity,Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Light] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X". 
            Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!" at this point the weapon does not perform at it's best.
            Weight: How much the item weights when equipped. 
            Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit
            Attack Type: Defines what kind of swing set the weapon has: Regular(R), Thrust(T), Slash(Sl), Strike(St)

                    I believe this might be the best shield for spear and/or rapier users who like to shield-thrust. Reasoning:

                    Shields in DS3 follow a clear pattern where shields that do not have 100% physical protection usually have a far higher stability to compensate. When player shield-thrusts - if he gets hit mid-thrust he loses health even if the shield has 100% phys protection AND a great deal of stamina on top of that. So, since we take damage anyway... Why not try to converse stamina instead, as to not get caught with no stamina at all?

                    For this reason, for shield-thrusting its far prefferable to use shields with high stability than high protection rates. And this shield is one of the best for that.
                    65 max stability, combined with an extremely decent 93% physical protection, combined with surprisingly low weight of 3,5 is an exceptionally good deal, all things considered.
                    The closest 2nd best is Wargod Wooden shield which weights 4.0 and provides 67 stability at +10, but less protection (and the suprise use of weapon arts).
                    After that its only greatshields.

                    Might try to make a low-SL character with this thing, see how it goes.
