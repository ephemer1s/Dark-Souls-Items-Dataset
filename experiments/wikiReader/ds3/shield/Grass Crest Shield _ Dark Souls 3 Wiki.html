            Grass Crest Shield is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            Old medium metal shield of unknown origin.

            The grass crest is lightly imbued with magic, which slightly speeds stamina recovery.

            Skill: Parry
            Repel an attack at the right time to follow up with a critical hit. Works while equipped in either hand.





            Notes and Tips:

            • Can NOT be Infused.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Provides an average damage block of 47.8%. Has poor damage block capacity, more of an auxilary tool than a shield due to its ability.
            • One of the crest Shields, each one specializes in a different niche.
            • Improves Stamina recovery by 4 points per second while equipped.
            • Does stack with Chloranthy Ring.
            • Stability 56/57 at +4/+5



            Location/Where to Find

            • Road of Sacrifices: From the Crucifixion Woods bonfire, drop down to the right of the large crab towards one of the large trees. Loot the corpse on the right side of the tree. (Watch the Item Location Video Guide)
