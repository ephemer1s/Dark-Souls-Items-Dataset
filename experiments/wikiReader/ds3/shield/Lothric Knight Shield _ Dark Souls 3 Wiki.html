            Lothric Knight Shield is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            Shield of the renowned Lothric Knights, decorated with the royal crest.

            The Lothric Knights contended with dragons, and their shields have suitably high lightning absorption befitting a dragonslayer's arms."

            Skill: Parry

            Repel an attack at the right time to follow up with a critical hit. Works while equipped in either hand.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Provides an average block of 70%.
            • Boasts the highest stability of all medium parrying shields: 66 stability at +10 upgrade level.
            • Infusing with a Lightning Gem at +10 upgrade level will result in a Lightning damage absorption value of 95.7% while guarding, the highest unbuffed value possible for all shields (narrowly leading the Dragonslayer Greatshield by 0.7%).

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by any Lothric Knight wielding this shield 
            • Sold by Greirat for 3000 souls (after sending him to scavenge at Irithyll Valley)
