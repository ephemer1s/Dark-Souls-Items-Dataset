            Porcine Shield is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            Shield based on a pig's head, depicted with unsettling authenticity.

            Shields such as these were made to shame weak-willed knights, and those who carried these shields were subject to merciless ridicule.
            No one will defend a craven who abases himself.

            Skill: Shield Bash
            Without lowering your guard, give the enemy a good shove with the shameful pig's snout.
            Works while equipped in either hand.





            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite. 
            • This shield was also featured in Dark Souls II. It could be obtained from chest located after Smelter Demon boss fight.
            • Dude it's a pig
            • Can be used as a sled with the Firekeeper in Firelink Shrine

            Location/Where to Find

            • Trade an Undead Bone Shard with Pickle Pee on top of Firelink Shrine.
            • Due to the fact that you lose one of your Undead Bone Shards in process of obtaining this shield, it is recommended that you don't sacrifice it before getting to NG+, where next Bone Shards become pretty much useless.
