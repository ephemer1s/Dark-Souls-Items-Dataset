            Elkhorn Round Shield is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.



            Standard round wooden shield. The blue antlers are reminiscent of designs found in Mirrah. Wooden shields are light, manageable, and offer relatively high magic absorption.


            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Not recommended for dedicated blocking, due to low absorptions and poor stability. 
            • Visually similar to Benhart's Parma from Dark Souls 2.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Sold by Greirat in Firelink Shrine for 1500 Souls.
