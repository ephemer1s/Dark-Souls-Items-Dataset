            Black Knight Shield is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            Shield of the Black Knights who roam the lands. A flowing canal is chiseled deeply into its face.

            Long ago, the black knights faced the chaos demons, and were charred black, but their shields became highly resistant to fire.

             

            Skill: Weapon Skill

            Equipping this shield in the left hand allows one to perform the Skill of the right hand weapon.





            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Highest stability of all medium shields.
            • Second highest Fire Defense after the Dragon Crest Shield in category Standard Shields.
            • Provides an average block of 73%.

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Black Knights. The first Black Knight is near the Farron Keep Perimeter bonfire. He can drop the entire Black Knight Set, in addition to the Black Knight Greatsword.
            • Can be farmed in the area after Champion Gundyr in the Untended Graves as it contains 4 Black Knights and is directly adjacent to the bonfire.
            • Another Black Knight is located in the Smouldering Lake near the bonfire Demon Ruins.
