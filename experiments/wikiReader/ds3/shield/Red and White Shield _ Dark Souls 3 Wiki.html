            Red and White Shield is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            Standard round wooden shield. It features a striking red and white design.  

            Wooden shields are light, manageable, and offer relatively high magic absorption.  

            Skill: Parry  
            Repel an attack at the right time to follow up with a critical hit. Works while equipped in either hand. 





            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be buffed, but can be infused.
            • Reinforced using Titanite.
            • NOT recommended for blocking, due to low defense, and poor stability. Due to its light weight and blessed infusion off the bat, it is a useful offhand parry tool.
            • One of the very few +1 shields found in-game.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted from a hanging corpse in Undead Settlement, in the pit near Irina. Must be shot down with a projectile. Is pre-infused with the Blessed infusion and +1.
