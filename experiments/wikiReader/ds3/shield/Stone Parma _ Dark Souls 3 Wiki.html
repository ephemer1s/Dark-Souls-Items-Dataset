            Stone Parma is a Shield in Dark Souls 3.




            A round, stone shield, once carried by an old knight who turned to stone himself.

            It has powerful defensive properties for its size, but is extremely heavy.

            Skill: Weapon Skill
            Equipping this shield in the left hand allows one to perform the Skill of the right hand weapon.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • While it is slightly outclassed in stats by the Silver Knight Shield (.5 lighter) and the Black Knight Shield (.5 heavier), this shield can be infused.  Simple will keep your weapon skill going for example.
            • Maximum stability is 63 at +9 upgrade level.
            • Provides an average block of 64%.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Can be found under a slug along the left wall just outside of the first Farron Keep bonfire.
            • Video location (fextralife video starting at 2h 24m 15s)
