            Ice Rapier is a weapon in Dark Souls 2.

             

            Rapiers wielded by sorcerers of Eleum Loyce, imbued with a magic that is released with a strong attack.

            A great protective wall was erected to protect Eleum Loyce from the encroaching chaos, and these sorcerers were amongst its defenders. 

            Acquired From

            • DLC: Crown of the Ivory King
            • Dropped by Rampart Soldier.

             

            Hints and Tips

            • Upgraded with Twinkling Titanite to +5.
            • Strong attacks unleash a bolt of ice, similar to a Soul Spear. Hitting an opponent at point blank will cause significantly more damage as you are also hitting with the weapon itself.
            • Strong attack can be parried if within range but the ice bolt can still damage the person parrying, it also neglects spell parry with the exception of Repel.
            • Loses very little physical damage upon elemental infusion and has the highest character sheet AR of any rapier, at the cost of losing the scaling.

                                  Kind of ambiguous in the description above - but the special attack is a magic projectile and infusing with magic will cause the damage of the projectile to increase. However, the damage of the projectile does not seem to be particularly affected by the actual level of the users intelligence (tested at 1 intelligence and 99 intelligence, each +0 rapier shot did roughly half of the health of the Scythe wielding jailers in the Lost Bastille). Damage also seems to be unaffected by dex scaling of the weapon as well, using similar test from above. Damage is increased slightly by magic clutch ring (~20 damage at +0). Upgrading weapon does increase damage, but not a ton (seems to be the theme in the DLC weapon, with almost all weapons having high base damage with minimal improvement to be gained from weapon upgrades)

                                  IMHO seems like the best way to utilize would be to get min stats and just stock 3 +0 magic ice rapiers in your left hand slot just to have a powerful, spammable ranged attack - Seems way cheaper than actually farming soul spears - and you can do it on any build with only 15 dex and 11 strength.
