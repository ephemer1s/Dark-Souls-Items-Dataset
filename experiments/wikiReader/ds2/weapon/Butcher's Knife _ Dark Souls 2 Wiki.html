            Butcher's Knife is a weapon in Dark Souls 2.

             

            An oddly large butcher's knife.

            Not your standard weapon, but certainly deadly enough to be utilized as one.

            Absorbs the HP of foes.

            Who in their right mind would create such a thing?

            Perhaps it is best not to dwell on how it was likely used in the past.

            Effects: HP absorb 

            Acquired From

            • Weaponsmith Ornifex, traded for the Soul of the Rotten and 5,000 souls.

             

            Hints and Tips:

            • Reinforced with Petrified Dragon Bone to +5.
            • Has regular axe R1s but unique R2s both one handed and two handed. Curiously, when powerstanced it applies a powerstanced greatsword moveset rather than an axe or greataxe moveset.
            • Has a unique one handed strong attack that looks similar to a lesser version of the plunging attack which can be used to generate impressive hit combos vs fleeing opponents.
            • The Butcher's Knife restores HP equal to 8% of the damage dealt by the weapon.
            • The shape of the Butcher's Knife may suggest that the axe type sweetspot doesn't apply to it, but it does in fact (axes do maximum damage at a certain range and less at any other range). But due to the fact that the weapon is quite long for a "light" axe, it's quite hard to hit outside the optimum range (only by hitting with the tip of the Butcher's Knife).
