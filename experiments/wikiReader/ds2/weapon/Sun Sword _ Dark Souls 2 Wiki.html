            Sun Sword is a weapon in Dark Souls 2.

             

            A well-forged long sword.

            Of unknown origin, and exhibiting no special power, but sturdily crafted.

            Perhaps it does not live up to its name, but nevertheless, merely wielding this sword seems to cause warmth to well up within one's bosom.

            Acquired From

            • Reward for reaching Rank 2 of Heirs of the Sun covenant. (Offer 20 Sunlight Medals)

             

            Hints and Tips:

            • Upgraded with regular Titanite to +10.
            • Like the Fume Sword, it has a full suite of thrusting attacks with the exception of the 2 handed R2, which is a slashing attack (roll attacks are still thrusting, however).
            • You can only acquire one Sun Sword per character as covenant progress does not reset with NG+ and beyond, unless another player drops their Sun Sword in your world.
            • When upgraded to +10 this weapon does 350 physical damage with 40 strength and 40 dexterity. 175 base + 210 with scaling.
            • The Sun Sword suffers greatly in parameter bonuses after infusion. For example: despite the lightning sun sword having a scaling rating of B and C on strength and dexterity, its physical scaling is actually worse than Lightning Heide Knight Sword's C and D (when both weapons at +10 are equipped at the same time, the Sun Sword offers less bonus physical damage than the heide sword). It is therefore discouraged to infuse this weapon, even mundane.
            • One-handed strong attack does a thrusting-type attack unlike most other straight swords.
            • Not to be confused with the miracle Sunlight Blade.
            • Trivia: It's based on Sunlight Straight Sword from Dark Souls, which was wielded by Solaire of Astora, its one handed strong attack is also a thrusting move.
            • The game incorrectly lists attack type as only Slash when it clearly has both Slash and Thrust attacks.

            Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity,Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.
            The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Light] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].
            This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".
            Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!" at this point the weapon does not perform at it's best.
            Weight: How much the item weights when equipped.
            Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit
            Attack Type: Defines what kind of swing set the weapon has.
            Regular(R), Thrust(T), Slash(Sl), Strike(St)

                                      Sooo for anyone curious:

                                      with 15 strength, 16 dexterity, Ring of Blades+1 and Flynn's Ring, Sun Sword+6 actually has an AR of ~308. Which is really not bad. (With 15/13 str/dex it's probably like 300 AR, started with a swordsman at 16 dex so idk)

                                      I'm guessing that after fully upgrading it I'll be rocking like ~340 AR with nearly minimum stats.

                                      The reason I'm doing that is because I'm playing a faith heavy hexer and need a physical weapon for DLC. So far, even with just the +6 sun sword, I'm doing significantly more damage than my dark and lightning shortswords. A lot of that is due to Flynn's and Blade rings scaling better to pure physical.

                                      Just finished Shulva at SL105 with above setup and it wasn't too bad at all. I'd recommend it to low weight faith characters with low str/dex as well as ones who have high str/dex.

                                      There are other weapons you can do this with, but the Sun Sword moveset and aesthetics are really solid. It weighs 3 units so you can throw on a bow, shield, and 2 catalysts without getting too heavy. Even at 15 str and 16 dex, the Sun Sword+6 scaling is +87.

                                      That said, Flynn's ring is sort of essential to make it work. It adds 50 AR. Can be found about halfway through Shulva.
