            Blacksmith's Hammer is a weapon in Dark Souls 2.

             

            An ordinary hammer used by a blacksmith.

            Can be used as a hammer, but not to any great effect, as it was made to shape weapons, not serve as one. 

            Acquired From

            • Given by Blacksmith Lenigrast if dialogue is exhausted and 8000 souls are spent on upgrades/reinforcements. (Majula).
            • Or dropped by Blacksmith Lenigrast if killed.

             

            Hints and Tips:

            • One-Handed regular (light) attacks are similar to axes.

                                    You see that part in the description where the game tells you this is an inefficient weapon? That's the game lying to you. At 50 Str and 7 Dex the damage of this weapon compared to the Mace is 375 to 369. That's not all that much more given the hammer's increased weight, but the increased poise damage is very useful. Yet where this weapon truly shines if when enchanted. The B scaling from physical damage gets moved to elemental damage, which really works in your favor if you meet the minimal requirements to wield the hammer. Plus, the base stats of an enchanted Blacksmith's Hammer are quite a bit higher than those of any other weapon in its class, making it very effective. If you want a single weapon with limited stat requirements on any caster build, this is a solid choice.
