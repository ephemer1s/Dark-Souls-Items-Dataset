            Aurous Set is an Attire or Armor in  Dark Souls 2. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their Builds.



            "Helm/Armor/Gauntlets/Leggings of kings of the desert land of Jugo. First worn by Aurous, the land's heroic founder.
            According to legend, the armor of Aurous is composed of a mysterious substance that cannot be seen by cowards. What do your eyes tell you? "

            Set Location

            • Spend 15k souls at Maughlin's shop in Majula, then come back with zero souls. Talk to him and he'll give you the Invisible set.
            • Dropped by dark spirit Rhoy the Explorer in Grave of Saints.
            • SotfS: The Gutter, in a chest near the Heide Knight (Non-Invisible Version).

             

             

             

            Notes

            • Upgraded with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Raises equipment load: helm & gauntlets +1%, chest and leggings +1.5%, 5% total.
            • Contrary to popular belief, it's impossible to make this set visible by ranking up in the Company of Champions covenant.
            • Armor becomes visible when aiming a bow. This can only be seen briefly as you change to first person view.
            • Armor becomes visible when rolling with the Simpleton's Ring. It can only be seen briefly at the beginning and end of the roll before you become invisible.
            • In addition to giving extra carry weight the whole set is incredibly light for the protection provided, essentially freeing up a lot of soul levels which would have otherwise been spent on VIT.

            /"Helm/Armor/Gauntlets/Leggings of kings of the desert land of Jugo. First worn by Aurous, the land's heroic founder.
            According to legend, the armor of Aurous is composed of a mysterious substance that cannot be seen by cowards. What do your eyes tell you? "

             

             

            Set Location

            • The entire visible set drops in different parts by a dark spirit in No-Man's Wharf that invades on the ship on NG+ and above, thought the chances are rare (spawns once).
            • [Scholar of the First Sin] Found in a chest near Heide Knight (wearing Iron Mask) in The Gutter. To find the knight, reach the first Swollen Mongrel past the first long bridge, turn to the opposite direction, cross the house and drop down.

             

            Notes

            • Upgraded with Twinkling Titanite.
