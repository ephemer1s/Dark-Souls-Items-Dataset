            B/E

            Grave Warden Set is an Attire or Armor in Dark Souls 2. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their Builds.







            Hood changed to: "Hood worn by a Grave Warden sorcerer. The man claims to have sealed himself away, but who's ever heard of sentient magic?
            It is no doubt difficult to judge the veracity of the man's words, but this hood certainly seems to possess an unusual power."
            "Robe/Gloves/Leggings worn by a Grave Warden sorcerer. He calls himself Navlaan, now."

             

            Set Location

            • Dropped by Grave Wardens and Earthen Peak and Undead Crypt.

             

            Notes

            • Upgraded with regular Titanite.
            • Robe offers second highest poison resistance (almost equal to that of Northwarder and Tattered Cloth robes).
            • The armor is reminiscent of the Ancient Egyptian clothing, which slightly resembles that of the connection between the Grave Wardens and Agdayne, Guardian of the Undead Crypt, because Agdayne wears clothing similar to those found in Ancient Rome, and Ancient Rome expanded its territory into Ancient Egypt during Augustus Caesar's reign.
