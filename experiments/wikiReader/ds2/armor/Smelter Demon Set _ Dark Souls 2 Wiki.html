            Smelter Demon Set is an Attire or Armor in Dark Souls 2. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their Builds.





            "Armor of the Smelter Demon, a great mass of iron that was given life. High fire defense."
            "Armor of the Smelter Demon, a great mass of iron that was given life. High fire defense. The Old Iron King was possessed of a great bounty of ore, but was incinerated by a creature that rose from the infernal depths of the earth."

             

             

            Set Location

            • Sold by Maughlin the Armourer (35,3k) once Smelter Demon is defeated (spend 15k souls at Maughlin's shop to unlock boss armor sets).





            Notes

            • Upgraded with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Upgraded stats are only 40% higher, not the usual 50%.
            • Helm offers second highest physical defenses (almost equal to those of Gyrm helm, regular Gyrm Warrior helm and Velstadt's helm), except for strike defense.
            • Gauntlets offer the highest fire defense (equal to that of Penal cuffs), as well as second highest physical defenses (except for slash defense) and second highest poise (almost equal to that of Looking Glass gauntlets).
            • Leggings offer second highest poise (almost equal to that of Looking Glass and Mastodon leggings).
            • Requires 16 STR.
            • While fire defense is indeed high, it's not the highest (except for gauntlets): among medium and heavy armor pieces, higher fire defense is offered by Syan's Helm, Alonne Captain's and Syan's armor and boots, all of them upgraded with easier to obtain regular Titanite.
