            Gundyr's Halberd is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Halberd of Gundyr the Champion, received when he was charged with his duty.

            This old cast-iron halberd has the power to break poise, and is said to never crumble, seeming to suggest that Gundyr was fated to eternal service from the beginning.

            Skill: Champion's Charge
            Hold spear at waist and charge at foe, and use momentum to transition into sweeping strong attack. 

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Buffed or Infused. 
            • Upgraded with Titanite Scales.
            • Despite the description saying it "has the power to break poise", its poise damage is no higher than any other Halberd.
            • Champion's Charge Weapon Art spin attack causes knockdown.
            • Is a weapon with a huge outlier for durability, standing at a whopping 500 durability! This equals to about 5000 hits before breaking.
            • Among the longest Weapons:
              • Dragonslayer Swordspear and Immolation Tinder are longer.
            • Despite being called a halberd, it more closely resembles a bardiche.

             

             

             Softcaps & Scaling:

            • Gundyr's Halberd +5
              • 2-Handed Stats
                • 30/15 = 463 AR = Base Stats & Dex Softcap
                • 30/40 = 483 AR
                • 40/15 = 481 AR = Str Softcap
                • 40/40 = 502 AR
                • 66/15 = 518 AR = Str Hardcap
                • 66/40 = 539 AR
            • Gets less than 2 AR per level after softcaps
            • Strength weapon
              • Dexterity Softcaps at base stats
              • Gains much from Strength than Dexterity
              • Softcaps at 40 Str
              • Scales poorly between 41 and 58 Str
              • Scales well between 59 and 66 Str



            Location/Where to Find

            • Craft via Soul Transposition from Soul of Champion Gundyr
