            Witch's Locks is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A whip fashioned from the black hair of a daughter of the Witch of Izalith, creator of the Flame of Chaos.

            The long, shining locks woven together to create this whip are imbued with the essence of fire, which can be channeled through the use of its skill. 

            Skill: Flame Whip
            Temporarily coat the whip in fire, the self-same fire as that which consumed the Witch herself, it is said.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed. 
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Like all the other whips, it cannot be parried but also cannot riposte, backstab, or plunge attack.
            • Like all whips it cannot be deflected off shields, walls, or buffed players. This allows for it to attack through walls.
            • Its innate fire damage and Weapon Art make it a good weapon for Pyromancers.

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Found inside a chest in a secret room behind a bookcase in Grand Archives. There is a lever you must pull in the same room (it is the very dark room after the first spot where you can dip your head in wax).
            • Temp Video Location (13m40s)
