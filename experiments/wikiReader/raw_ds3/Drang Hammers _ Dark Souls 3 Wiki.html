            Drang Hammers is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Paired hammers of the Drang Knights, descendants from the land known for the legend of the Linking of the Fire.

            When the Drang Knights disbanded, they scattered across the lands as sellswords. They quickly became known for shieldless, aggressive tactics that struck fear in the hearts of men.

            Skill: Spin Bash
            Bash foes with a large spinning motion, and utilize momentum to transition into an overhanded strong attack smash.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • When dual-wielding, knockdown or stun occurs after the second hammer lands.
            • R1 > L2 > R2 and L1 > L2 > R2 are true combos. 



            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted at the Cathedral of the Deep: next to one of the chained-Giants. It is slightly hidden underneath a set of wooden staircases. 
            • Watch the Fextra Video at 2hr 37mins 43secs in.
