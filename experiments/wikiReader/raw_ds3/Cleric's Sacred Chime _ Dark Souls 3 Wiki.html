            Cleric's Sacred Chime is a Chime in Dark Souls 3.



            Sacred chime for casting miracles of the Gods. Chimes such as these are often given to clerics who become Undead.

            Equip a talisman or a sacred chime to cast miracles. Miracles must be attuned at a bonfire before use.

            Skill: Gentle Prayer
            Recovers HP for a period of time, albeit extremely slowly. Works while equipped in either hand.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • At 60 FTH it has a spellbuff of 232.
            • This chime is the strongest between 29-35 Faith OR 44-49 Faith.
            • The Priest's Chime is superior between 10-28 Faith OR 36-43 Faith.
              • Priest's Chime is equivalent to this chime with 34-35 Faith.
            • Yorshka's Chime is superior with 50 Faith or more.
            • Gentle Prayer only heals as long as it stays in your hand.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Starting equipment for the Cleric class.
            • Dropped by Undead Clerics at the Cemetery of Ash. Farm the mobs from the bonfire up to the boss area.
            • Also drops from the same enemy in Untended Graves.
