            Drang Twinspears is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Paired spears of the Drang Knights, proclaimed descendants from the land known for the legend of the Linking of the Fire. When the Drang Knights disbanded, they scattered across the lands as sellswords. They quickly became known for shieldless, aggressive tactics that struck fear in the hearts of men.

            Skill: Charge

            Hold spear at waist and charge at foe. Use strong attack while charging to extend the length of the charge. 

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be infused and buffed.
            • At +10 and 40/40 (STR/DEX) this weapon deals less damage when infused with any physical damage infusions. That is, it deals more damage uninfused than it does infused with Heavy, Sharp, Raw, or Refined.
            • Reinforced using Titanite.
            • Has the same rolling L1/LB attack has Gotthard Twinswords when 2-handed. However it did not recieve the same nerf Gotthard's did.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by the Drang Knight wielding the spears in Irithyll of the Boreal Valley, after the Pontiff Sullyvahn bonfire.

Do NOT play this weapon infused with any elements, you have a great lack of damage in comparison to buff or straight dex. As for combos, this weapon can pull new ones out of its ass left and right. You obviously can do your r1-&gt;r1, l1-&gt;l1, and r1-&gt;l1, but this weapon has more than just that. You can use r2-&gt;l1 for great mixups. My preferred combo is r1-&gt;l1-&gt;r2, but the r2 can easily be parried so you need to know you opponent ahead of time, and if you can't go for that and they roll backwards, go for a jumping attack instead. The main difference here is that you need to play unlocked, or it will never hit, but does nasty damage and is very easy to land when you learn your angles. In my use, I have yet to find a use for the weapon art, but who knows if it might have a place here. This is my current favorite weapon, and it always makes me sad to see nobody use it.
