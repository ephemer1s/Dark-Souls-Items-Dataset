            Carthus Shotel is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Strangely curved sword crafted to cause bleeding, wielded by Carthus swordsmen.

            Shaped to sneak around the defenses of shields, this sword requires ample dexterity to wield effectively.

            Skill: Spin Slash
            Slice into foes with a large spinning motion, and continue spinning to transition into strong attack.



            Notes and Tips:

            • The Shotel has the moveset of Scimitar, Carthus Shotel has the moveset of Falchion.
            • Can be Buffed and Infused
            • Reinforced using Titanite.
            • Resembles an Egyptian Khopesh. Though an Egyptian Khopesh is much smaller and has the cutting edge on the opposite side of the blade.
            • Has innate shield pierce: 65% on heavy attacks, 35% on the first and second spin of the weapon skill, 55% on the last spin of the weapon skill, and 15% on all other attacks.



             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Skeleton Swordsman in Catacombs of Carthus that wield this weapon.
