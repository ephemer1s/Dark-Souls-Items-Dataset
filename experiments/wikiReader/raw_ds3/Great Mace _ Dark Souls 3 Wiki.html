            Great Mace is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Large mace wielded by the knights of the Cathedral of the Deep.

            The extremely heavy strike attacks of this weapon are devastating and will break weaker shields with a single blow. Wielding such a weapon will require inhuman strength.

            Skill: Perseverance
            Anchor weapon in earth to temporarily boost poise. Damage reduced while activated.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed. Reinforced with Titanite.
            • At 40 Str a Heavy Great Mace +10 has a 1-handed AR of 573.
            • At 66 Strength, a +10 Heavy-infused Great Mace becomes the third strongest Great Hammer. The second strongest being Smough's Great Hammer and the strongest is  Ledo's Great Hammer with 627 AR.
            • Of the elemental infusions, Lightning is the optimal path in terms of AR. With 40/40 Str./Faith a Lightning Great Mace +10 has 722 AR, two-handed.


            Trivia

            • Appears to be an Italian-style of mace, or closely resembles one.

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped from Cathedral Knights wielding this weapon (easily farmed from the Dancer of the Boreal Valley bonfire, up the ladder and to the left).

                                                                                                                                                            But what about the Dragontooth? At +5 it gets 642 AR at 66 STR/two-handing/can be buffed to 737 AR with resins/has Perseverance/has a passive that raises magic &amp; fire absorption by 10.                                                                                                                                                             Well, here's something the stat list DOESN'T tell you about the Great Hammers: Stamina efficiency.

                                                                                                                                                            At 40 Endurance + Ring of Favor: One bar of stamina gets you 5 swings with the Great Mace.

                                                                                                                                                            At 40 Endurance + Ring of Favor: One bar of stamina gets you 4 swings with the Dragontooth.

                                                                                                                                                            And the +3 Ring of Favor doesn't change anything. The Great Mace still has higher AR, lower weight, and significantly less stamina consumption than the Dragontooth. Even Leo's Great Hammer struggles to beat the resin-buffed DPS of the Great Mace.
