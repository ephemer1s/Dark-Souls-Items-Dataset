            Sacred Chime of Filianore is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3. It was added with The Ringed City DLC.



            A sacred chime blessed by Princess Filianore, matriarch of the church.

            Filianore's favor knows no boundaries, and this sacred chime expands the range of vicinity-affecting miracles that heal, cure, or provide other enhancements.

            Skill: Pray for Favor
            Works with chime equipped in either left or right hand. Heals HP very slowly for a duration of time. Affects caster and those in vicinity.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Pray for Favor
              • Regenerates 4 hp/s for 60 s
              • Total = 240 hp
              • Remains active when the chime is put away, unlike Gentle Prayer.
            • Spell Buff at +5 Upgrade
              • 105 - 20 Fth
              • 132 - 30 Fth
              • 159 - 40 Fth
              • 182 - 50 Fth
              • 200 - 60 Fth
              • 211 - 99 Fth
            • Increases the range of AOE healing Miracles.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Given by Shira in The Ringed City when you first speak to her while she is behind the doors at the top of the large steps. Accept her request to kill Darkeater Midir and she will give this.
            • If you talk to Shira, knock Midir off the bridge, then touch Fillianore without returning to Shira then Shira will leave her home. She will now be found on the right of Fillianore's Rest. Defeating her yields you the Sacred Chime of Fillianore, Titanite Slab, and her Shira armour set which is found at her original location. Doing her quest is NOT required since this route makes it impossible to complete.

                                                                                                                                  Did some tests on the healing power of the Sacred Chime of Filianore+5.
                                                                                                                                  My charcter has 99 faith, and I am wearing the Morne's ring and Ring of the Sun's First Born. The Spell Buff on the chime is 211. Also my character has 50 Vigor (1689HP when embered). Tested in firelink shrine by killing myself from fall damage with tears of denial.

                                                                                                                                  Heal Aid: 317 HP
                                                                                                                                  Heal: 635 HP
                                                                                                                                  Med Heal: 869 HP
                                                                                                                                  Great Heal: 1165 HP
                                                                                                                                  Soothing Sunlight: 1483 HP

                                                                                                                                  For comparison, here are the numbers with Yorshka's Chime+10, Spell Buff of 263, same two rings.

                                                                                                                                  Heal Aid: 395 HP
                                                                                                                                  Heal: 791 HP
                                                                                                                                  Med Heal: 1081 HP
                                                                                                                                  Great Heal: 1450 HP
                                                                                                                                  Soothing Sunlight: 1689 HP (a full heal)

                                                                                                                                  And then Sunlight Talisman+10, 214 Spell Buff, same two rings

                                                                                                                                  Heal Aid: 322 HP
                                                                                                                                  Heal: 644 HP
                                                                                                                                  Med Heal: 880 HP
                                                                                                                                  Great Heal: 1181 HP
                                                                                                                                  Soothing Sunlight: 1503 HP

                                                                                                                                  So it is a decent amount weaker than Yorshka's and just a bit less than Sunlight, I suppose they did this as the range of the heals is noticably larger. Also note, I tried testing it with the Rose of Ariandel Buff, and the WA does not affect healing miracles, only offensive miracles.
