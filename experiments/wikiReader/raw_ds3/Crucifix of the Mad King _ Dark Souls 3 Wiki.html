            Crucifix of the Mad King is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3. It was added with The Ringed City DLC.



            A cross spear hung with a malformed corpse. 

            Once, a mad king was born to the pygmy royalty and Shira, knight of Filianore, put him to rest. But Shira's cross spear, unable to kill the undying king, only pinned itself to him. Shira delivered them together to the darkroom, where she stayed and held them close.

            Skill: Mad King's Folly
            Slam malformed corpse into the ground to briefly awaken it, then fillet with strong attack to trigger a roar, a blessing, or vexation.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Upgrade Material: Twinkling Titanite.
            • Infusable: No
            • Buffable: Yes
            • Despite it's innate dark damage, Dark Blade does work on this weapon, increasing its damage even further.
            • Both physical and dark damage scale with Faith.
            • It has the Great Club R2. Convenient for groups of enemies or catching rolls.
            • When the weapon art is performed, it will randomly trigger one of three effects:
              -Roar is an AoE blast that covers your front and sides.
              -Blessing works similarly to Perseverance.
              -Vexation spawns a following floating sphere, which explodes dealing Dark damage to you and all enemies (it can be dodged).
            • Shira never triggers the vexation effect when she's using this weapon.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Drops from Shira when you face her in the final area of The Ringed City DLC.
