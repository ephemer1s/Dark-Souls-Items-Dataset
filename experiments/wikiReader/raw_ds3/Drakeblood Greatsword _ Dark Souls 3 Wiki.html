            Drakeblood Greatsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Greatsword wielded by an order of knights who venerate dragon blood.

            This sword, its blade engraved with script symbolizing dragon blood, inflicts magic and lightning damage.

            Skill: Stance
            While in stance, use normal attack to break a foe’s guard from below, and strong attack to slash upwards with a forward lunge.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • If infused with Dark or Chaos, both Magic and Lightning damage will scale with Intelligence and Faith, respectively.
            • In infused with Blessed, its base damage and Lightning damage will also scale with Faith.
            • If infused with Simple, its magic damage will scale with Intelligence.
            • The lightest Greatsword weapon, and it's the same length as the Bastard Sword.
            • This weapon has the same two-handed attack chain as the Hollowslayer Greatsword.
            • Keep in mind each damage type is separately calculated by an enemy's respective defence and absorption stats, so the listed AR needs to be taken with a pinch of salt. It is recommended to focus on maximising one damage type, either with infusions or buffs, even if the listed total AR attained is lower.
            • Defenses are harsh on low ARs (i.e. 70), but reward medium-to-high amounts of AR.
              70 AR may transform into an additional 10 damage per swing under normal circumstances, but 150 AR may add about 50 damage.
            • The Drakeblood Greatsword has a range of 31.5838xl on the 1h R1 and 35.229xl on the 2h R1.



             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by the Drakeblood Knight in Archdragon Peak, near the Dragon-Kin Mausoleum bonfire.
