            Red Hilted Halberd is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Long-hilted weapon mixing spear and axe. The red-hilted halberd is associated with clerics.

            Can be thrust like a spear or swept sideways like an axe, making it a versatile weapon effective against multiple foes.

            Skill: Perseverance
            Assume an unfaltering stance of prayer to temporarily boost poise. Damage reduced while activated.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • It with 40 STR/40 DEX and infused with Refined, it has 480 AR.



            Location/Where to Find

            • In the sewers of Undead Settlement, next to where you find the statue of Velka. (Watch the Fextra Video)

                                                                                                Whether you use this or the vanilla Halberd is mostly a preference of which Skill you like more. The real-world AR difference between both Halberds on any given build / infusion is about 2~10 AR, with RHH being the slightly higher one. They're almost identical outside of the Skill.

                                                                                                Perseverance allows you to momentarily Poise through attacks that would usually interrupt you, so your attack will go through even if you're hit during your animation. Perseverance also provides a massive physical damage-reduction buff for an instant when you use the skill, which allows you to win trades against heavy weapons by just ignoring some of the damage with the buff.

                                                                                                The Charge skill of the vanilla Halberd allows you to quickly close distance and deal some follow-up damage, the total damage will be significantly higher than a basic R1, but isn't quite as spectacular as Champion's Charge (which also knocks enemies down), but at least the regular Halberd is buffable. You can also use this move to trade as it has a lot of built in hyper-armour, but it will generally be less effective than Perseverance unless your Charge interrupts the opponent's action.
