            Carthus Curved Greatsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Large curved sword wielded by swordsmen of Carthus.

            The lightest of the curved greatswords. Crafted to cause bleeding.

            Skill: Spin Slash
            Slice into foes with a large spinning motion, and continue spinning to transition into strong attack.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Inflicts bleed damage.
            • At +10, infused with Blood and with 40 Luck, it will have 99 Bleed.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Grave Warden Skeletons in the Catacombs of Carthus.
