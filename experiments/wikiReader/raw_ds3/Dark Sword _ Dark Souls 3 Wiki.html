            Dark Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Pitch-black straight sword of the Darkwraith, survivor of the land swallowed by darkness.

            The Darkwraiths were the first red orb invaders, and originators of a unique sword technique inspired by their thick, broad blades.

            Skill: Stomp
            Use one's weight to lunge forward with a low stance and increased poise, and follow with strong attack for an upward slash.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • All weapons have motion values assigned to each attack, which are modifiers to AR that are associated with the attack being used.
              • The Dark Sword has a motion value of 200 on backstab, 250 on riposte, and 60+225  on Hornet Ring riposte. The motion value of a backstab with a straight sword does not change with Hornet Ring.
            • The Dark Sword has a range of 35.0199xl on the 1h R1 and 34.6102xl on the 2h R1.
            • The Dark Sword has a unique, vertically slashing R2, both 1h and 2h.
            • The only straight sword in the game to have the Stomp Weapon Skill.
            • When infused with a Dark Gem, it will be called a "Reinforced Dark Sword" instead of "Dark Dark Sword."
            • There is a glitch where a Dark-infused Dark Souls could be buffed with Dark Blade, which some dubbed a Dark Dark Dark Sword.

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by:
              Darkwraith [FK] 8.00%
              Darkwraith [HWoL] 8.00%
            • The Farron Keep Perimeter Bonfire is next to two Darkwraiths that will wander into and fight a group of Ghru. If left to play out the Darkwraiths will usually survive with minimal HP - this allows the player to farm them with little risk.
