            Heysel Pick is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Choice weapon of Yellowfinger Heysel, a Finger of Rosaria and xanthous scholar.

            It`s both a weapon and sorcery staff. The gold specks are remnants of lost sorceries.

            Skill: Steady Chant
            For a short moment, boost the strength of sorceries.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused but can be Buffed by using Steady Chant.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Functions as a weapon and a catalyst for Sorceries.
            • At 60 INT it has a spellbuff of 208 when fully upgraded.
            • Due to doubling as a catalyst, this weapon has no heavy attacks, but can perform a jumping attack.
            • Using Steady Chant both increases the damage of the spells and the weapon itself.
            • Can true combo into Soul Greatsword or Old Moonlight, provided the player's cast speed is fast enough.
            • Also combos into Great Soul Arrow, Farron Flashsword, and Great Farron Dart, but not anything equivalent to or slower than Heavy Soul Arrow.
            • Can technically combo into Deep Soul and Great Deep Soul, but you need to stand close.



             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by invading dark spirit Yellowfinger Heysel in Road of Sacrifices.
            • Video location (Fextralife video starting at 43m 58s)
            • Alternatively, will drop from the worm-like enemy in Rosaria's room if it appears.

                        27 Apr 2021 15:43  

                        Outside of true combos or such, I think this weapon has nice PvP potential. Since it's a pure Thrust weapon, every physical attack can benefit from the Leo Ring, which makes trading with straight swords more punishing. The 2H R1 stuns pretty well, and with its mixed damage, it's harder to reliably block the damage. Also, since it's a catalyst, you can cast a homing soulmass to force opponents into rolling while you ready another, heavier spell like Soul Greatsword or Soul Spear. This also benefits from fighting parry spammers, since a whiffed parry gives you time to cast and react. And finally , the WA buffs both the spells and the melee, so you can sacrifice a bit of FP for essentially a reusable Pale Pine Bundle.

                        Don't sleep on the Heysel Pick, folks; it's a great multi-tool with a medium-high skill ceiling. Fighting with it has been some of the most fun I've had in a while.
