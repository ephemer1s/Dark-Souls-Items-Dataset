            Darkmoon Longbow is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Longbow of Darkmoon Gwyndolin, who was gradually devoured by Aldrich.This golden bow is imbued with powerful magic and is most impressive with Moonlight Arrows.

            Skill: Darkmoon Arrow
            Infuse a readied arrow with Darkmoon essence, granting it magic damage and the ability to pierce shields.

            Location/Where to Find

            • Transposable from Soul of Aldrich for 5,000 souls.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Buffed or Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite Scales.
            • Often considered by many to be the worst bow in the game. It's magic damage, even when fully upgraded, is too low to be considered useful, it's arguably worsened by the split damage, and the only way to get any real damage is with the Moonlight Arrows.
            • Damage may vary depending on ammunition(arrows).
