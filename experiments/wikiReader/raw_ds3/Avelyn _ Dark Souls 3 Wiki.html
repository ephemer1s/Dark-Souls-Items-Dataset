            Avelyn is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            An extremely rare rapid-firing crossbow.  

            Fires three successive bolts by means of an elaborate mechanism. Inflict heavy damage by making all three shots count.  

            Despite its use as a weapon, this crossbow is also a priceless work of art, and it bears resemblance to a musical instrument. 

            Skill: Tackle
            Lunge into a shoulder tackle, pushing back enemies to create distance. 

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Buffed or Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Is severly outclassed in terms of raw damage and range, beating beaten out by every Crossbow by 100+ AR and 10-20+ range. However, the Avelyn has the advantage of firing off 3 times in quick succession, making it very effective at close range and a nice quick and easy damage dealer
            • Individual shots of the burst are weak, however if all three shots land on the target it deals the most damage of any Crossbow with every ammo type. 

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Found in the Grand Archives. Climb the first shortcut ladder or pass through the very dark room where you can also find Power Within. Then go up the first flight of stairs and across the balcony with a broken railing at the end where a single Scholar is standing. Drop down onto the bookshelf below and then roll off these onto bookshelves even further below. (video location)
