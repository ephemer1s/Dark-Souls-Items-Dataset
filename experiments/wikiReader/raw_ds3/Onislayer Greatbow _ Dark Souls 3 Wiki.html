            Onislayer Greatbow  is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A unique greatbow handed down in an eastern land, where tales are told of its use in slaying giant horned oni.

            Drawing a bow of this size takes time, and leaves the user vulnerable. Only specialized great arrows can be fired from the bow.

            Skill: Puncturing Arrow
            Masterfully fires off a great arrow that pierces through all enemies in its path, inflicting damage.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • It's range value is 63, the highest range for any kind of bow.
            • Since bows cannot be used without being 2-handed, the "true" Strength requirement for this weapon is 12.
            • This greatbow deals less damage than other greatbows, but it's strength resides in it's sniping capabilities.
            • Scaling is weird on this weapon.
              • Strength bonus peaks at 27 Strength (18 by two-handing).
              • Dexterity peak scaling bonus is reached at 36 Dex.
              • It softcaps at 40 Dex.
              • You get mediocre scaling up to 55 Dex.
              • Very bad scaling up to 75 Dex.
              • And relatively good scaling up to 99 Dex.
              • Leveling up from 12 to 18 Strength provides better damage than going above 40 Dexterity.
              • Optimal stats: 18 Strength 40 Dexterity.
            • Resembles the Alonne Greatbow from Dark Souls 2.
            • Damage may vary depending on ammunition(great arrows).



            Location/Where to Find

            • Profaned Capital - Drop off on the bridge leading from the bonfire to the Yhorm boss fight.
