            Caitha's Chime is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Sacred chime blessed by Caitha, goddess of tears.

            Rare even amongst Carim clerics. Affected by intelligence, a rare thing for miracle catalysts, and also agreeable with miracles that lean towards the Dark. Its existence is concealed in the name of the Archbishop, as it is anathema.

            Skill: Gentle Prayer
            Recovers HP for a period of time, albeit extremely slowly. Works while equipped in either hand.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed. 
            • Can only cast Miracles.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Dark miracles cast with this chime will scale with both Intelligence and Faith up to a 45/45 softcap. Dark miracles cast with any other chime will scale only with Faith up to 60, and will deal only less damage.
            • Contrary to this chime's description, its Intelligence scaling only applies to dark miracles and will have no effect on miracles that deal lightning or physical damage
            • When casting normal miracles with this chime, only the Faith scaling is considered and its softcap is at the normal amount of 60 Faith.
            • The chime has to remain active in your hand for the weapon art to continue regenerating health.
            • At 60 INT and 60 FTH it has a spellbuff of 312.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Lothric Castle
              • Start at Dragonslayer Armor Bonfire
              • Go back towards the entrance to the bossfight and turn left. Before you should be a chapel with an elevator in it
              • Enter the chapel, then go up the first flight of stairs within
              • Turn left to find an open doorway and use it to exit the chapel
              • Once on the balncony, turn left to see a break in the guard rail
              • Very slowly walk off the balcony through the break. Fall damage in Dark Souls 3 scales with your maximum HP, therefore a higher amount of HP is not required, however the Silvercat Ring and the Spook sorcery will help if you are not interested in taking fall damage.
              • Upon landing, turn left and make your way to the end of that section of roof. You have a very small drop to make. It should not cause fall damage. Drop down.
              • Pick up Caitha's Chime from the corpse on that ledge.



            Trivia:

            • When using Caitha's Chime with Lightning Arrow, the 'bow' part of the model can be misaligned.
