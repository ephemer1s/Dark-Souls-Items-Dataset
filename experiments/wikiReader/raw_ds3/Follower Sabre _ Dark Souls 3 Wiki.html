            Follower Sabre is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3. It is part of the Ashes of Ariandel DLC.



            Great curved sword wielded by Farron Followers. Its great weight enables heavy-handed chain attacks.

            The Followers brandish their swords to hunt warriors taken by the Abyss. Their technique is honed to face men, implying those yet to lose their human aspect are their likeliest prey.

            Skill: Prying Wedge

            A chain attack that wedges below foes and swings upward, breaking their guard. Strong attack can be used to follow up the onslaught with a fatal blow.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Upgraded with Titanite.
            • Uses Falchion move set.
            • Has 401 AR when infused with Refined and 40 Strength and 40 Dexterity. 
              • When infused Sharp and with 18 Strength and 60 Dexterity it has 405 AR.
            • Retains the basic kick rather than the somersault typically associated with curved swords.



             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Painted World of Ariandel: From the Ariandel Chapel bonfire, leave the chapel and attack the bridge causing it to fall and become a ladder. At the bottom of the ladder, follow the ridge to the first root you must traverse; on your left is another root with a Follower sitting, drop on it and follow it to the end. At the end of that root, you should see another root under you on your right hand side, drop down on that root and once more on the next one. You should see an area with a crystal lizard and a follower. Continue past him onto a small root which leads to another small area and a javelin Follower. You should see 2 roots, one that descends and one that ascends, follow the ascending root then on your right hand side fall onto a root that has the Follower Sabre.
