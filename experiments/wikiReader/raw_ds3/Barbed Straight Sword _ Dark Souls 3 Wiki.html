            Barbed Straight Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Sword of Longfinger Kirk, the infamous Knight of Thorns. This sword's blade is lined with countless deadly thorns.

            The thorns of this ominous weapon induce heavy bleeding.

            Skill: Stance
            While in stance, use normal attack to break a foe's guard from below, and strong attack to slash upwards with a forward lunge.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • All weapons have motion values assigned to each attack, which are modifiers to AR that are associated with the attack being used.
              • The Barbed Straight Sword has a motion value of 200 on backstab, 250 on riposte, and 60+225 on Hornet Ring riposte. The motion value of a backstab with a straight sword does not change with Hornet Ring.

             

            • The Barbed Straight Sword has a range of 32.7816xl on the 1h R1 and 32.3289xl on the 2h R1.
            • The Barbed Straight Sword has thrusting R2 attacks.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Dark Spirit Longfinger Kirk in Cathedral of the Deep.
