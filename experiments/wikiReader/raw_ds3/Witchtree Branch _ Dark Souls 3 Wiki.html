            Witchtree Branch is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            The branch of a large, well-tended witchtree, used as a sorcery catalyst.

            Witchtree staves are customary in the far north, and allow for faster casting than ordinary catalysts.

            Skill: Steady Chant
            Boost the strength of sorceries for a very short period. Works while equipped in either hand.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Spellbuff:
              • 60 Int = 184
              • 50 Int = 168
              • 40 Int = 149
              • 30 Int = 127
              • 20 Int = 104
              • 18 Int = 100
            • Provides a passive casting speed buff that is stronger than Sage Ring +2 (+99 Virtual Dexterity, but the cap is 50 Dexterity so it doesn't make a difference for most builds)
              • Buff applies for all spells you cast from all schools, even those cast with another catalyst in your other active slot.
              • Buff only applies while Witchtree Branch is in an active slot (ie. in your hand or on your hip).
              • You do not have to meet Int requirements for the buff to apply

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted from corpse behind a locked gate in Irithyll of the Boreal Valley. Must destroy an illusory railing in order to acquire it. (Railing Location Video)
