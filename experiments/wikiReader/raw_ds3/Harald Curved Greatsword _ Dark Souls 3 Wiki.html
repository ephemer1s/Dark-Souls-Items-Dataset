            Harald Curved Greatsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3. It is part of the The Ringed City DLC.



            Giant gold-decorated curved sword wielded by warriors of the Harald Legion, who sought the dark soul.

            The swords sank into the dark with the legion, where their blades were severely corroded.

            Skill: Sever
            Hold the giant blade with both hands and slash repeatedly at foe's feet.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Outclassed by the Exile GS in damage with most infusions at meta level, only outclasses Exile GS with sharp infusions, therefore is better used for it's unique qualities (weapon art and range).



            Location/Where to Find

            • The Ringed City: From the Earthen Peak Ruins bonfire, head over the castle and roots into the swamp. On the left end of the swamp, under the flying hollow, you will find a cave-like area under a root with a large hollow. You can find the greatsword on a corpse here. Video Example
