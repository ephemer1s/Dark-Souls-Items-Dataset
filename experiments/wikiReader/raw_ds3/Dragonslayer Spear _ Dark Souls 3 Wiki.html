            Dragonslayer Spear is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Cross spear associated with Ornstein the Dragonslayer. A weapon of the gods imbued with the strength of lightning.

            Two-handed thrust utilizes the support of the cross and requires great might, but can pierce deep into the flesh of dragons, and send mere men flying.

            Skill: Lightning Charge
            Charge with spear at waist to enwreathe with lightning, then release bolts with final thrust. 



            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.. 
            • The description refers to the second attack of the heavy combo, although it does not seem to actually throw people.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • A great pseudo combo with this weapon is R1 > R1 > R2 while two handed. 
            • The WA > R2 is not required to deal the full damage from the WA. 

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • From the Dragon-Kin Mausoleum bonfire in the Archdragon Peak area, go up the stairs and it is on the grid right in front of you.
