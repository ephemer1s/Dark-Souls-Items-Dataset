            Shortsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Small straight sword excelling in thrusting attacks.

            The shortsword is a light and easily wielded straight sword, due to its minimal attribute requirements.

            Skill: Stance
            While in stance, use normal attack to break a foe's guard from below, and strong attack to slash upwards with a forward lunge.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • All weapons have motion values assigned to each attack, which are modifiers to AR that are associated with the attack being used.
              • The Shortsword has a motion value of 220 on backstab, 275 on riposte, and 66+247.5 on Hornet Ring riposte. The motion value of a backstab with a straight sword does not change with Hornet Ring.
            • The Shortsword has a range of 32.2247xl on the 1h R1 and 31.7071xl on the 2h R1.
              • sp, or Shield Pokes, are a unit of measurable distance to determine the range of a weapon. Certain actions create different distances from a wall, allowing one to test the range of certain attacks. While this testing isn't 100% accurate, it is a very good indicator of the ranges of each attack.
            • The Shortsword has thrusting R2 attacks.
            • One of only two Straight Swords in the game with 110 critical modifier, the other being the Lothric Knight Sword.
            • The Shortsword is good on casting builds due to its light weight, low stat requirements, and versatile moveset. Furthermore, it infuses very well, making it good on all elemental builds, and due to its light weight it takes less stamina to swing.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Sold by Shrine Handmaid.in Firelink Shrine for 600 souls
