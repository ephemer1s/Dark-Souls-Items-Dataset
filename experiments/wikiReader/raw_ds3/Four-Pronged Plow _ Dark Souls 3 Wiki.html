            Four-Pronged Plow is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            This four-pronged plow is shaped like a fist, and is a worker's tool in the Undead Settlement.

            Not originally intended for battle, but serves as a deadly weapon owing to its sharp points.

            Skill: Charge
            Hold spear at waist and charge at foe. Only, it is important to have good footing. 



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • The Weapon Art is slightly different from the regular Charge - extending it via a heavy attack will make you trip and fall, and against other players you can easily be backstabbed afterwards.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by undead wielding this weapon in Undead Settlement.
            • Temporary video: 2h 13m timestamp
