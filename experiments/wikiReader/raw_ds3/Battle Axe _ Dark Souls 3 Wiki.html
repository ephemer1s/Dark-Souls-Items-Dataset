             Battle Axe is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Easily-wielded axe crafted for battle and inflicting standard damage.

            It's weight can be used to inflict high damage, but must be used carefully as it leaves its wielder open to retaliation.

            Skill: Warcry
            Let out a spirited warcry that temporarily boosts attack.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Starting equipment for the Warrior.
            • A Deep Battle Axe can be found on a mimic behind the fire-breathing dragon in the High Wall of Lothric.
