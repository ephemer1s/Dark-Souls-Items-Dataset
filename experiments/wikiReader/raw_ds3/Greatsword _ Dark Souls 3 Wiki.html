            Greatsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            This ultra greatsword, with its thick blade, is one of the heaviest of its kind.

            Highly destructive if intolerably heavy.

            There would appear to be some credence to the rumors that this sword tested the true limits of human strength.

            Skill: Stomp
            Use one's weight to lunge forward with a low stance and increased poise, and follow with strong attack for a spinning slash.

             

            Notes and Tips

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite. 
            • Gets bested by the Black Knight Greatsword on a 40/40 build. BKG has 612 AR, Refined Greatsword has 590 AR. However, the Greatsword can be buffed.
            • The Greatsword benefits greatly from Hollow and Raw infusions.
            • Does poorly with physical infusions on standard quality builds.
            • A Hollow Greatsword with 60 STR/60 Dex/45 LCK has 673 AR.
              A Heavy Greatsword with 60 STR/10 DEX has 619 AR and should
              always be better than hollow infused considering the massively lower
              stat cost and comparable damage.
            • The longest Ultra Greatsword in the game.
            • 66/65/94 on STR/DEX/LCK has 701 AR (731 AR two-handed).
            • This weapon is a reference to the Dragonslayer, the iconic sword wielded by Guts from the manga series Berserk.
            • 28/10 - STR/DEX - 446 AR 1h, 502 AR 2h - Min Reqs
            • 40/10 - STR/DEX - 500 AR 1h, 522 AR 2h - Str Softcap
            • 60/10 - STR/DEX - 522 AR 1h, 545 AR 2h - Str Softcap
            • 99/10 - STR/DEX - 556 AR 1h, 556 AR 2h - Str Hardcap
            • Works well with Dark/Chaos infusions
            • 28/10/30/30 - STR/DEX/INT/FTH - 235/471 AR 1h, 264/471 AR 2h, - Min Reqs + Dark/Chaos Softcaps
            • Dispite being named "greatsword" it is in fact an Ultra Greatsword and not from the Greatsword class, although its moveset is roughly the same as the Bastard Sword, while greatly exaggerated. 

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted from a corpse on top of a broken bridge in Farron Keep. Go straight ahead from the first bonfire until you reach a broken bridge, look behind you and you will see another piece of broken bridge and a body holding an item, that item is the Greatsword (video location).
