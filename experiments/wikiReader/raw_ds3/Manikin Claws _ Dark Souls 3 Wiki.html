            Manikin Claws is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Weapon of the Pale Shades, assassins of the Sable Church of Londor. The curved claws cause heavy bleeding.

            When two handed, claws are equipped to each hand.

            Skill: Quickstep
            Instantly step behind or around the side of foes. Especially effective when locked on to target.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Can be used to parry attacks when equipped in the left hand.
            • Has the most range out of the Claws but is also the weakest.

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Dropped by Londor Pale Shade that invades you in Irithyll of The Boreal Valley right after the Silver Knights room (The invader will only show up if you're on bad terms with Yuria - i. e. kill the pilgrim in the Church to save Anri). 
            • Dropped by Dark Spirit Londor Pale Shade that invades you in the Farron Keep, on the right side near the wall, right after the Basilisk. (Must be on bad terms with Yuria of Londor. I.E. Cured the sigil, attacked, or killed Yuria.)
            • The Manikin claws, Londor Pale Shade set, AND duel bow gesture, can all be obtained in one playthrough by punching Yuria 3 times to aggravate her, killing the invading Londor Shade in Farron Keep and Irrythil (BEFORE KILLING PONTIFF), and then paying for absolution at the statue of Velka in the undead settlement. The Londor Shade will still be summonable, Yuria will be friendly, so you can receive all the items without discontinuing the questline.
