            Painting Guardian's Curved Sword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A weapon described in legends passed down amongst heretics, wielded by the Painting Guardians.

            A uniquely shaped weapon with a flat tip.

            Skill: Chained Dance
            Execute relentless consecutive attacks while tracing a circle in a unique dance of deadly grace.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Upgrades with Titanite
            • When held in the left hand, pressing L2/LT will preform a parry.
            • It is the shortest Curved Sword in the whole game.
            • R1, R1, L2 is a rollcatching combo against players.



            Location & Where to Find

            • Found on a corpse in the beams just below Company Captain Yorshka in Anor Londo
