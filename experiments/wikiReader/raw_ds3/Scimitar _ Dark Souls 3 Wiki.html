            Scimitar is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Small curved sword that excels in swift movements and consecutive attacks.

            The scimitar's flesh-rending slash atacks are greatly compromised against armor and tough scale-covered hides.

            Skill: Spin Slash
            Slice into foes with a large spinning motion, and follow with strong attack for a spinning vertical-slash.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Upgrades with Titanite.
            • Two scimitars are wielded by the red knight figurine of the collector's edition.
            • Can be used to parry when held in the offhand weaponslot. 

             

            Softcaps and Scaling

            • 7/13 = 216 = Base Stats
            • 7/40 = 331 = Dex Softcap
            • 7/99 = 395 = Dex hardcap
            • After softcaps it gets less than 2 AR per level
            • Dex weapon, receives negligible strength scaling



            Location/Where to Find

            • Sold by the Shrine Handmaid at Firelink Shrine for 600 souls.
            • Dropped by Scimitar wielding skeletons in Catacombs of Carthus.
