            Morning Star is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Hammer with a sharp spike on its pommel. One of the more barbaric cleric weapons.
            The sharp thorns allow this strike weapon to induce bleeding.

            Skill: Perseverance
            Assume an unfaltering stance of prayer to temporarily boost poise. Damage reduced while activated.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced using Titanite.
            • Unless on a unique build the Mace tends to be a direct upgrade in damage, the damage being so bad even despite it having A scaling in sharp unlike the Mace it does less damage.(Note that this was tested with softcaps.)



            Location/Where to Find

            • Sold by Shrine Handmaid for 2500 souls after obtaining Paladin's Ashes.

                    Okay, so having recently tried this on an SL60 +6 Dex invader, I can safely say this is one of my favourite weapons in post-Pontiff. Perseverance on the rafters, hammer rollcatches, and relatively light weight are all shared between Mace and Morning Star. That said, what makes me choose this over the Mace most of the time is the bleed build-up. Sacrificing ~6? AR for bleed is worth it when it continues to pressure your opponent, since that little status bar keeps the psychological pressure going and pushes less confident opponents to make more mistakes or panic. Persevering through some Ultras (with decent armour, of course) and knocking someone into panic rolls builds up bleed, and you can start your rollcatch chain from there if you have the stamina. Actually managing to get the bleed pop is icing, but don't count on it. Use the build-up for its pressure and mind games, and don't be afraid to change weapons if the situation calls for it.

                                                          Having done a bit of messing about with Mugenmonkey, a bit of info on infusions, as of the last time Mugenmonkey was updated...

                                                          With 15 Str/10 Dex/40 Int/40 Fth, AR (one-handed) for some infusions are as follows:

                                                          Crystal - 458
                                                          Simple - 411
                                                          Lightning - 476
                                                          Blessed - 304
                                                          Chaos/Dark - 478

                                                          Oddly, even with a Pyro build, Lightning almost outperforms Chaos and Dark.

                                                          With 15 Str/10 Dex/60 Int/10 Faith,

                                                          Crystal - 473
                                                          Simple - 425

                                                          Meanwhile, with 15 Str/10 Dex/10 Int/60 Fth,

                                                          Lightning - 491
                                                          Blessed - 320

                                                          Mind, both 60/10 and 10/60 free up 10 more levels that can be spent elsewhere.

                                                          The long and short of these findings is that the Morningstar has oddly above-average compatibility with the Lightning infusion, and even poorer compatibility with the Blessed infusion than most weapons, from what I can tell.
