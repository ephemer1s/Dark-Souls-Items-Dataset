            Crow Talons is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.  It is part of the Ashes of Ariandel DLC.


            Talons used by Corvian Knights. Inflicts five perpendicular slashes, causing heavy bleeding.

            In their infatuation with Sister Friede, the Corvian Knights swore to protect the painting from fire and to this end, too to the execution of their own brethren.

            Skill: Raptor Flurry

            Spread right and left talons like a broad set of wings and launch into a tornado of consecutive attacks.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforce with Titanite.
            • There is a glitch that can be used wherein the Crow Talons WA can prevent all fall damage, even if it would prove fatal with the Silvercat Ring equipped. 
            • Has 354 AR at 15/50 STR/DEX when Infused with Sharp.
            • When two-handing it has a unique L1/LB running and backstep attack.
            • Two Handing changes roll and R2/RB attacks.



             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Found on the rooftops in the Corvian Village guarded by 2 magic-wielding Corvians

                                                                                                                                                                                      I used the Crow Talons for a very long session of PvP and another after that and here's what I picked up:
                                                                                                                                                                                      - The rolling L1 doesn't turn all the way when locked on your target, might hit with the second but not the first unless you adjust the aim manually (its decent at roll catching people that are moving backwards though, if you rolled into them and then they roll in fear of a rolling attack for example).
                                                                                                                                                                                      - The running L1 hitbox is questionable. You're lucky to hit with one claw, if even with that. I've had people (PvP) straight up not dodge and the attack has missed completely, without them rolling or anything. You have to be lined up directly at them for both claws to hit, PvE and PvP which will almost never happen. Its mostly just extra pressure/fear factor. They should've given it light Hyper Armor like they did with the Crow Quills since it takes a moment to bring down the slash.

                                                                                                                                                                                      Personal overall opinion = Its the most damaging claw weapon, with 16/60 stat spread for STR/DEX its attack rating is 168+192 = 361 (which is higher than on Refined 40/40 163+183 = 347). It has a few unique moves compared to the other claws and the WA completely shreds people if you hit the full thing. Has hyper armor on said move to boot which I've used to charge directly into Greatswords and still beat the trade no question.
                                                                                                                                                                                      It doesn't replace Claw or Manikin Claws. It serves a different purpose. Claw has its Leaping Slash which arguably is easier to hit with, Manikins are for mobility and Crow Talons are a god damn blender. The WA absolutely melts HP in PvE and PvP alike. So its mostly a gimmick weapon to set the Raptor Flurry into action. That's all folks. I'd suggest using it, good fun.
