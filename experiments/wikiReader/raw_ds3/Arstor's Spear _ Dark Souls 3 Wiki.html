            Arstor's Spear is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            One of the curses that festered within the belly of the Greatwood, and a terrible weapon favored by Earl Arstor the Impaler.

            The spear is enwreathed in rotten, heavily poisonous meat. Defeating foes restores HP.

            Skill: Shield Splitter

            Take a large step forward and make a single focused thrust to puncture enemy shields and inflict damage.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed. 
            • Reinforced with Titanite Scales.
            • While equiped Arstor's Spear will cause the wielder to receive 30HP whenever an enemy dies. This happens regardless of whether or not the player is present for the killing. 
            • Stacks additively with Ring of the Evil Eye allowing for upwards of 60HP per kill - effective against large groups of weaker enemies including Deacons of the Deep.
            • Most likely belonged to Sir Arstor of Carim, mentioned in Dark Souls 1's Bloodbite Ring, Cursebite Ring, and Poisonbite Ring description. Is also credited for creating Purging Stones, and the Shotel.

             

            Softcaps & Scaling:

            • 11/19 = 273 AR = Base Stats
            • 40/19 = 313 AR = Str Softcap
            • 11/40 = 358 AR = Dex Softcap
            • 40/40 = 397 AR = Quality Build

             

            Location/Where to Find:

            • Soul Transposition from Soul of the Rotted Greatwood for 1,000 souls.

                Something those who don't play DS1 might not notice:
                Earl Arstor is from Carim. In fact, Arstor has a lot of ties to curses and statuses in general.
                He's the one that commissioned the Bite rings be made, and the Purging Stones of DS1 that take curses off the player are Arstor's secret treasures.
                From the way he's talked about, he's alive during the events of DS1, and so the fact his name lasted for so long into DS3 is crazy.

                It seems he was a knight at some point before becoming the Earl, as in DS1's bite rings' descriptions it calls him "Sir Arstor of Carim". Then he evidently became the Earl, and began creating and secretly keeping Purging Stones. After the events of DS1, he picked up this spear and went on a mad rampage, earning the name "Earl Arstor the Impaler".

                  Arstor's Spear is secretly amazing for lower level invasions, just don't tell anyone. I have a low level Fetid / Poison Knight build who uses Arstors Spear, Executioners Greatsword, and Ring of the Evil eye +3 in one of the loadouts for HP/FP Regen. I taunt hosts and phantoms chase to me throughout the stage and whittle them down with poison and pokes while they clear the weak enemies. I always outtrade them since I get ludicrous healing with that set-up. I use toxic mist and dung pies and just try to slowly chip them away until they die. Throw on Story-tellers staff and the poison damage you will proc adds up fast.

                  Its especially fun to invade in the graveyard in Cathedral of the Deep with the infinite respawning zombies. You get 60HP back per enemy that dies in the stage, don't even need Estus on that character if you play it right. The poison will kill them slowly, and it almost always makes phantoms blindly chase you into the a bad situation since the damage isn't too terrible. Its a spear, and spears are more utility than being your main source of damage. Love this spear :)
