            Mace is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.

            Iron hammer designed for use in battle. A common weapon for clerics.

            This bladeless strike weapon is effective against most foes, and can break the guard of a shield.

            Skill: Perserverance
            Assume an unfaltering stance of prayer to temporarily boost poise. Damage reduced while activated.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • This weapon has the Mace moveset. Because of this, doing three (two-handed) R1s can connect on a PvP opponent, with the third R1 acting as an easy roll-catch. 

            Location/Where to Find

            • Starting equipment for the Cleric
            • Sold by Greirat after you bring him to Firelink Shrine, or from Shrine Handmaid after giving her Greirat's Ashes, for 600 souls.
