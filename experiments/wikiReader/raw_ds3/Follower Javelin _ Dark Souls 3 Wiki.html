            Follower Javelin is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3. It is part of the Ashes of Ariandel DLC.



            Light spear wielded by the Farron Followers. Balanced to allow for throwing.

            The Followers attack in groups, surrounding foes, shielding themselves, and thrusting their spears at their foes. On a final command, they hurl their spears at their fallen foe, to give the retired warrior an honorable send-off.

            Skill: Hurl Spear

            Emulate the practice of the Followers by throwing an illusory spear that assumes temporary form.



            Notes and Tips:

            • Weapon can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Has A and B scalings for the elemental infusions making it a decent option for Pyromancers, Sorcerers, and Faith users.
            • Has a bug that prevents it from preforming a rolling attack after a one-handed leaping attack like most weapons can, rather it cannot attack at all after performing a leaping attack.



            Location/Where to Find

            • From the Snowfield Bonfire, run to the right towards the ice trap. After falling down the ice trap, hug the right wall to avoid the wolves aggroing. After a little bit of walking, you will encounter a path up guarded by a wolf. Kill it quickly before it howls and attracts more. Up at the very top of the path, there is a group of about 5 Farron Followers who will all aggro you at once if you get near, so prepare for a fight. Behind them is a corpse holding the Javelin, and a drop to return you back to the Bonfire.
