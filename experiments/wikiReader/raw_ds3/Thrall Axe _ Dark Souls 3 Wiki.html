            Thrall Axe is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Small hand axe used by Lothric slaves.

            A cunning weapon for a cunning lot, this axe is quick and deadly.

            Skill: Quickstep
            Instantly step behind or around the side of foes. Especially effective when locked on to target.

             



            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Infused and Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Drops from Hollow Slaves (Thralls) in Undead Settlement, Cathedral of the Deep and Grand Archives.
