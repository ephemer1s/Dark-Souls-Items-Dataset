            Millwood Battle Axe is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3. It is part of the Ashes of Ariandel DLC



            Battle axe wielded by Millwood Knights. Its blade is blessed by the symbol of the Ethereal Oak

            A hefty axe normally wielded by the mightiest of warriors.

            Skill: Warcry

            The unique warcry of the Millwood Knights entailed a leap straight toward the enemy and a fearsome roar.

             

            Notes and Tips:

            • Can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Upgraded with Titanite.
            • Amazing when used on a Strength build since it's buffable and has an A scaling in Strength when infused with a Heavy Gem.
            • The weapon art lunge can be handy for heavy or overburdened character since the leap can be used regardless your Equipment Load.



            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted from a corpse at the top of the broken Millwood tower. From the first bonfire (Snowfield bonfire), go down to the ice trap and into the forest full of wolves. After killing the wolves, go down to the edge and follow the path to the left, you will enter an open area with ruins and wandering Millwood Knights, the tower is unmissable. Enter the tower and climb the ladders and climb the stairs until you get to the very top of the tower. From here you have to drop to a plank then fall down to a dead Millwood which the battle axe will be on.
