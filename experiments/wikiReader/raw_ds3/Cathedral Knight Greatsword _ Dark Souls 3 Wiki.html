            Cathedral Knight Greatsword is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            Ultra greatsword wielded by the knights of the Cathedral of the Deep.

            Highly destructive if intolerably heavy.

            Its blade is flattened for use as a strike weapon, but the point at the end allows for thrusting.



            Notes and Tips

            • Can be Buffed and Infused.
            • Reinforced with Titanite.
            • Notably deals Strike damage with most attacks.
            • Deals Thrust damage with it's two-handed R2.
            • Its rolling attack is a spin, having notable area coverage around the player.
            • While two handed, a true combo for this weapon is R1 to R2



            Location / Where to Find

            • Dropped by greatsword-wielding Cathedral Knights found in either the Cathedral of the Deep or the Consumed King's Garden
            • For easy farming: Start at the Deacons of the Deep bonfire. Head back out of the boss room and up the stairs. On the other side of the large throne there is a knight wielding this weapon.

            Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity,Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat].             Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!" at this point the weapon does not perform at it's best.
            Weight: How much the item weights when equipped. 
            Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit

                                                                                                                                                              After 50+ hours using this weapon, I came to the conclusion that this is the BEST physical weapon in the game under/around SL (Level) 100. Several points below: Damage: Medicore. Other UGS can out dps this sword, but only this and the Fume can deal Strike damage with its attacks. Moveset: Amazing. One-handed horizontal and Two-handed vertical attacks, plus Two-handed poke makes this weapon very adaptable and can easily punish any enemy attack, plus counter damage. Weapon Skill/Art: Medicore. Stomp never was the most effective WA, especially not the horizontal one but it is still great for countering attacks from combo weapons like the Farron UGS and Ringed Knight PUGS. Strike/Thrust damage combination: This weapon is effective against armored opponents. All knights and many bosses are weak to Strike damage, and those that are not are weak to Thrust damage, making this weapon effective all opponents. How to get this weapon: It is a damn grind. Farmable, but the drop rate is very low. Method: Kill Deacons of the Deep, then from that bonfire repedatly kill the Greatsword Cathedral Knight before the bossroom. Stats at weapon level +10 40 STR Heavy Infused Two-handed: 566 Attack Rating. Stats at weapon level +10 66 STR Heavy Infused Two-handed: 617 Attack Rating.
