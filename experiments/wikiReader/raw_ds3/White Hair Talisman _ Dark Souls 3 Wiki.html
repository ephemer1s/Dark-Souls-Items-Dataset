            White Hair Talisman is a Talisman in Dark Souls 3.



            Talisman made from a lock of white hair.

            This lock of hair belonged to a deformed member of the chaos witches, mothers of the art of pyromancy. Fashioned into a talisman, it enables the wielder to cast both miracles and pyromancies.

            Skill: Combustion
            Creates a powerful flame in the wielder's hand. One of the most basic pyromancies, and for this reason, the flame created is fierce. Works while equipped in either hand.

            Acquired From

            • Found in the Smouldering Lake.
              • From the Old King's Antechamber bonfire, go through the illusionary wall on the far side of the room that drops down into the hall of fire orbs and pyromancer demons. Follow the far wall left to the end of the room; you should see a corridor with a rat in it.
              • You may follow the rat and drop into a lava pit to pick up the item, as well as the Toxic Mist pyromancy. Alternatively, take the first door on your right into a root-infested catacomb with several rats. One of the far walls is illusionary; grab the Quelana Pyromancy Tome if you haven't already and the stairs down into the lava pit. Mind the lava slimes.



            Notes and Tips

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Can cast Pyromancies and Miracles
            • Even though this talisman scales with both Intelligence and Faith, it does very little damage when casting dark spells. It is better suited to casting normal pyromancies and miracles.
            • Either due to a bug or by design, this talisman's Intelligence scaling will not apply to any pyromancies cast with it unless your Intelligence stat is equal to or higher than your Faith stat.
            • The talisman at +5 has a break point in damage inflicted by fireball, fire orb, Great chaos fire orb and black fire orb at 25 intelligence: the aforementioned spells get a sudden 30-40% boost, the same way the pyromancy flame gets a 90% boost on the same spells at 18 intelligence (video demonstration). Note that all other pyromancies are unaffected by this breakpoint, including Chaos Bed Vestiges despite its relative similarity with the orb-like spells affected by the breakpoint.
            • The weapon art Combustion is noticeably weaker than the pyromancy flame at same stats: damage dealt is around half.
            • Miracles cast with this talisman will only scale with Faith, and gain nothing from Intelligence. Dark miracles will scale with both, but they will be abnormally weak.
            • Ultimately less potent than a properly upgraded pyromancy flame in terms of damaging spells, making this talisman better suited to casting buffs such as Power Within or Carthus Beacon, alongside more standard miracles.
            • At 60 INT and 60 FTH it has a spellbuff of 200.

             

             

             

             

             

            Tips for Traversing the Lava

            • Once your fire resistance is high enough, you'll be able to drink Estus faster than you'll lose health.
            • Use any combination of high fire resist armor, Flash Sweat pyromancy(+30%), Red Bug Pellets (+15%), Flame Stoneplate Ring (+13%), Speckled Stoneplate Ring (+5%) Blue Tearstone Ring (+20% at 20%). 
            • In the End you should get around 66% resistance (73% with Blue Tearstone Ring)

             

             

            Lore

            • The description of the item, belonging to a "deformed member of the Chaos Witches", highly suggest that these white hairs could belong to the Fair Lady from Dark Souls I. The fact that this talisman can be used for miracles (basically healing magic) might be a reference to her attempting to heal Eingyi.
            • When viewed up close during casting animations where your character's hand is open, you can see the Old Witch's Ring from Dark Souls 1 on it. Image link: http://i.imgur.com/PCU9NkR.jpg

                                                                                                                                                                                                                    There's either a bug with this item specifically, or with two-handing the pyromancy Sacred Flame, something that is only capable with this catalyst as you can't two-hand the Pyromancy Flame.Equip the White Hair Talisman on your left hand and your strongest weapon on your right hand. Two-hand the White Hair Talisman (hold the two-handing button). Cast Sacred Flame on your poor guinea pig. You'll notice that Sacred Flame's upticks will act like R1 hits from your right-hand weapon, even taking the element of it. This is almost a guaranteed instant kill on most enemies and pretty much is instant kill for any and all players, even if they have Tears of Denial active.I'm uncertain if any other Pyromancy (or even Miracles) are affected by this bug. So if you see a guy two-handing a White Hair Talisman, fight at range and don't get caught.
