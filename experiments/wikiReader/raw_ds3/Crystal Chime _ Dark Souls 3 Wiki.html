            Crystal Chime is a Weapon in Dark Souls 3.



            A sacred chime, once the possession of Gertrude, the Heavenly Daughter, and defiled by the scholars of the Grand Archives.

            The power of crystals granted the scholars a degree of success. In this case, their work enabled this chime to be suitable for casting both miracles and sorceries.

            Skill: Gentle Prayer
            Recovers HP for a period of time, albeit extremely slowly. Works while equipped in either hand.

            Notes and Tips:

            • Cannot be Infused or Buffed.
            • Can be used to cast both Miracles and Sorceries.
            • Reinforced with Twinkling Titanite.
            • Spellbuff:
              • 45/45 = 223 = Softcap
              • 40/40 = 216
              • 30/30 = 202
              • 20/20 = 157
              • 18/18 = 148
            • This chime gets dual Intelligence and Faith scaling on all magic sorceries and all lightning miracles, but the physical miracles will only scale with Faith.
            • It does give dual Intelligence and Faith scaling to dark spells, however it doesn't give boosted damage to these like the Izalith Staff, Caitha's Chime, or Sunless Talisman do.
            • Weapon buff spells and Healing spells do not gain dual scaling with the Crystal Chime.
              • All sorcery weapon buffs will scale with only Intelligence
              • All miracle weapon buffs will only scale with Faith, including Dark Blade.
              • All healing miracles will only scale with faith
            • The chime has to remain active in your hand for the weapon art to continue regenerating health.
            • Weighing 1.5 less than a Sorcerer's Staff, it is the lightest sorcery catalyst in the game.
            • As it can be used without meeting its own INT/FTH requirements, it is ideal for casting spells that do not rely on scaling, such as Blessed Weapon, Force, Spook, or Snap Freeze.

             

             

            Location/Where to Find

            • Looted from Grand Archives, in a room behind the spear knight in the same area as the first wax pool

                                                                                                                              Part of that's because Sunless Talisman is meant more for dark spells.

                                                                                                                              Although, with those spells you'll get a whole lot more out of a normal Talisman than you would out of the Crystal Chime. Crystal is meant for offensive lightning and magic spells (its double scaling only applies to those, not to buffs), and Sunless only applies its double scaling to offensive dark spells (not buffs, just stuff like Dorhy's Gnawing or Lifehunt Scythe). A normal Talisman will get a lot more out of Great Heal, Lightning Blade, or Dark Blade than either the Crystal Chime or Sunless Talisman. Though, you won't be able to cast Hidden Body, I suppose.

                                                                                                                              This is one of those cases where the spellbuff value is highly misleading. It'll tell you the total scaling the catalyst gets from all stats, which makes catalysts with dual Int+Fth scaling look really strong on paper, however the full scaling will only apply to a select few spells so in practice they tend to be quite a bit weaker. Also, in most cases the spells that benefit from dual scaling are very weak without it, so all that really does is puts them on par with normal spells cast by a normal catalyst.
